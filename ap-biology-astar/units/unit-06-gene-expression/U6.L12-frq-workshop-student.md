## 一、序列题套路 / Sequence Questions

**给 DNA 写 mRNA/蛋白，三查**：
1. **方向**：标清 5'→3'；模板链 3'→5' 读 → mRNA 5'→3'
2. **配对**：转录 A→U（不是 T）；翻译用密码子表从 **AUG** 起读
3. **突变**：定位改变 → 判断类型（沉默/错义/无义/移码）→ 推蛋白后果

---

## 二、生物技术数据题套路 / Biotech Data

**凝胶电泳判图**：
- DNA 负电 → 向正极跑；**小片段跑得远**
- 用 ladder 估大小；比较条带模式找匹配

**PCR**：n 轮 → 2ⁿ 倍；耐热 Taq 酶（因每轮 95°C）

## 三、专训题集 / Practice Set

**Q1.** *[Conceptual Analysis — 中心法则｜4 分]*

A gene's template strand reads 3'-TAC-ACC-GAT-ATT-5'.

(a) **Write** the mRNA sequence (5'→3'). *(1)*
(b) Using the start codon, **determine** how many amino acids the polypeptide contains. *(1)*
(c) A mutation changes the template strand to 3'-TAC-ACC-**T**AT-ATT-5'. **Identify** the type of mutation and **predict** its effect on the protein. *(2)*

---

**Q2.** *[Analyze Data — 凝胶电泳｜4 分]*

DNA from a mother, a child, and two possible fathers is cut with a restriction enzyme and run on a gel. (Imagine the child's bands are a combination of the mother's bands and Father #1's bands.)

(a) **Explain** how gel electrophoresis separates DNA fragments and why DNA moves toward the positive electrode. *(2)*
(b) **Explain** how you could determine the biological father from the banding patterns. *(1)*
(c) **Explain** why a child's banding pattern should contain bands found in BOTH biological parents. *(1)*

---

**Q3.** *[Conceptual Analysis — 调控｜4 分]*

(a) **Explain** how the lac operon allows E. coli to express lactose-digesting genes only when lactose is present. *(2)*
(b) **Explain** how a eukaryotic cell can permanently silence a gene through DNA methylation. *(1)*
(c) **Explain** how two cells with identical DNA can become different cell types. *(1)*

---

## 四、自评要点（简答）

> Q1: (a) 5'-AUG-UGG-CUA-UAA-3'；(b) 从 AUG 起：AUG(Met)-UGG-CUA-UAA(stop) → **2 个氨基酸**（UAA 终止不计）；(c) 模板 GAT→TAT 使 mRNA CUA→**AUA**... 实际此改变使密码子由 UGG 后的 CUA 变化——重点：单碱基替换=点突变；若产生终止密码子则为无义突变，蛋白提前终止。
> Q2: (a) DNA 负电向正极，小片段跑远，按大小分离；(b) 孩子的非母源条带应与生父匹配；(c) 孩子一半 DNA 来自母、一半来自父，故条带来自双亲。
> Q3: (a) 无乳糖时阻遏蛋白结合 operator 阻断转录；有乳糖时诱导剂使阻遏蛋白失活→转录；(b) 启动子甲基化抑制转录且可遗传给子细胞；(c) 差异基因表达（转录因子+表观遗传）。
