## 📌 一句话本质 / In One Sentence

---

## 一、PCR（聚合酶链式反应）/ Polymerase Chain Reaction

**作用**：在体外**快速扩增**特定 DNA 片段（从极少量复制出数百万份）

**三步循环**（每轮 DNA 翻倍）：
1. **变性 Denaturation**（~95°C）：加热使双链分开
2. **退火 Annealing**（~55°C）：引物（primers）结合目标序列两端
3. **延伸 Extension**（~72°C）：**Taq 聚合酶**（耐热）合成新链

> 🔑 用**耐热聚合酶（Taq）**，因为每轮要加热到 95°C（串联 U3.L03 酶变性——普通酶会被煮坏，Taq 来自耐热菌）。
> n 轮循环 → 2ⁿ 倍扩增。

---

## 二、限制酶 / Restriction Enzymes

- **限制酶（restriction enzymes）**：识别**特定 DNA 序列（识别位点）**并在该处切割
- 例：EcoRI 识别 GAATTC 并切割
- 切割产生**黏性末端（sticky ends）**或平末端，可用于拼接 DNA（接 L11 重组 DNA）
- 来源：细菌（防御噬菌体的天然机制）

> 🔑 同一限制酶在不同 DNA 上切割位点数不同 → 产生不同大小的片段 → 可用电泳区分（指纹）。

---

## 三、凝胶电泳 / Gel Electrophoresis ⭐

**作用**：按**大小**分离 DNA 片段

**原理**：
- DNA 带**负电荷**（磷酸基，接 U1.L02）→ 在电场中向**正极**移动
- 凝胶像分子筛：**小片段移动快/远，大片段移动慢/近**（受阻更多）
- 加入染料显示条带（bands）

**读图**（FRQ 高频）：
- 离加样孔（负极端）**越远 = 片段越小**
- 用已知大小的 **DNA ladder（标准）**对照，估算未知片段大小
- 应用：DNA 指纹、亲子鉴定、基因型分析

> 💡 **记忆**：小快大慢——小分子跑得远。

## 四、应用 / Applications

- **DNA 指纹/法医**：比较不同样本的条带模式
- **疾病诊断**：检测致病等位基因
- **亲子鉴定**：比较条带共享

(A) cut DNA at specific sites  (B) amplify (make many copies of) a specific DNA segment
(C) separate DNA by size  (D) translate DNA into protein

**A2.** In gel electrophoresis, DNA fragments move toward the positive electrode because DNA is
(A) positively charged  (B) negatively charged  (C) neutral  (D) hydrophobic

**A3.** In gel electrophoresis, the SMALLEST DNA fragments
(A) move the slowest and stay near the well  (B) move the fastest and travel the farthest
(C) do not move  (D) move toward the negative electrode

**A4.** Restriction enzymes function to
(A) amplify DNA  (B) cut DNA at specific recognition sequences  (C) separate DNA by size  (D) translate mRNA

(A) DNA is heated to ~95°C each cycle, which would denature an ordinary enzyme
(B) Taq works only at low temperatures  (C) Taq cuts DNA  (D) Taq is negatively charged

### Part B · 进阶 MCQ

(A) Sample A's fragments are larger  (B) Sample A's fragments are smaller
(C) Sample A has no DNA  (D) the samples are identical

**B2.** After 3 cycles of PCR starting from one DNA molecule, approximately how many copies would there be?
(A) 3  (B) 6  (C) 8 (2³)  (D) 9

**B3.** Two individuals' DNA samples are cut with the same restriction enzyme and produce different banding patterns on a gel. This difference is BEST explained by
(A) the enzyme not working  (B) differences in their DNA sequences (different restriction sites), producing different fragment sizes
(C) DNA being positively charged  (D) identical genomes

### Part C · 真题风格 FRQ

(a) **Explain** how gel electrophoresis separates DNA fragments by size, including why fragments move and in which direction. *(2)*
(b) **Explain** how the scientist could use the banding patterns to identify which suspect's DNA matches the crime scene. *(1)*
