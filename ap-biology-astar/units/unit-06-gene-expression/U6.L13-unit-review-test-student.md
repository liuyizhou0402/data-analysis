## 一、必记对比表

| 对比 | 关键区别 |
|------|----------|
| 复制 vs 转录 | DNA→DNA(双链,两模板) vs DNA→RNA(单链,一模板,A-U) |
| 模板链 vs 编码链 | mRNA 与编码链同(T→U)、与模板互补 |
| 外显子 vs 内含子 | 保留表达 vs 剪掉 |
| 原核 vs 真核调控 | 操纵子 vs 多层次+表观 |
| 沉默/错义/无义/移码 | 不变/换1个/提前终止/阅读框全乱 |
| 体细胞 vs 生殖细胞突变 | 不遗传 vs 可遗传 |

---

## 二、易错点总温习

| 知识点 | 正确版本 |
|--------|----------|
| 转录配对 | A 配 U（不是 T）|
| 翻译起点 | 从 AUG 开始读，不是 mRNA 第一个碱基 |
| 后随链不连续原因 | 聚合酶只能 5'→3'，反向平行 |
| DNA 甲基化 | 通常抑制表达 |
| 电泳方向 | DNA 负电→正极；小片段跑远 |
| 分化 | 选择性表达基因，非丢失基因 |

## 三、单元测评 / Unit 6 Assessment

> 建议时间：55 分钟（含序列题 + 生物技术数据题）

### Section I · MCQ（10 题，每题 1 分）

**1.** The Hershey-Chase experiment demonstrated that
(A) protein is the genetic material  (B) RNA carries the code
(C) DNA is the genetic material  (D) ribosomes make DNA

**2.** DNA replication is described as
(A) semiconservative  (B) conservative  (C) dispersive  (D) random

**3.** During transcription, the base that pairs with adenine in the DNA template is
(A) thymine  (B) guanine  (C) cytosine  (D) uracil

**4.** Introns are
(A) non-coding regions removed during RNA processing  (B) coding regions that are translated
(C) the same as exons  (D) start codons

**5.** The start codon AUG codes for
(A) a stop signal  (B) uracil  (C) methionine  (D) glycine

**6.** In the lac operon, when lactose is present it acts as an inducer by
(A) activating the repressor  (B) binding RNA polymerase
(C) cutting the DNA  (D) binding the repressor so it can't bind the operator

**7.** DNA methylation of a promoter typically
(A) activates the gene  (B) silences the gene  (C) deletes the gene  (D) translates the gene

**8.** A mutation that inserts one nucleotide into a coding sequence causes a
(A) silent mutation  (B) no change  (C) frameshift mutation  (D) chromosomal duplication

**9.** In gel electrophoresis, smaller DNA fragments
(A) travel farther toward the positive electrode  (B) travel less far
(C) move toward the negative electrode  (D) do not move

**10.** Two cells in your body with identical DNA differ because of
(A) different genomes  (B) different chromosome numbers  (C) mutations in one cell  (D) differential gene expression

### Section II · FRQ（2 题，共 8 分）

**FRQ 1.** *[Central Dogma｜4 分｜建议 12 分钟]*

A DNA template strand reads 3'-TAC-GTA-CCG-ATT-5'.

(a) **Write** the mRNA sequence transcribed from this template (label 5'→3'). *(1)*
(b) Using the start codon, **determine** how many amino acids the resulting polypeptide will contain (the codon ATT on the template gives a stop codon UAA). *(1)*
(c) A mutation deletes the first nucleotide of the second codon. **Identify** the type of mutation and **explain** its likely effect on the protein. *(2)*

**FRQ 2.** *[Biotechnology｜4 分｜建议 12 分钟]*

(a) **Describe** how PCR amplifies a specific DNA sequence, naming the three steps of each cycle. *(2)*
(b) **Explain** why a heat-stable polymerase (Taq) is required for PCR. *(1)*
(c) **Explain** how gel electrophoresis could be used to compare DNA samples from two individuals. *(1)*
