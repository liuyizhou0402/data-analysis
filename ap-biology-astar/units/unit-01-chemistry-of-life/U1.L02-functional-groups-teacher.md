## 易错点 Misconceptions

| 学生常错 | 正解 | 纠正 |
|----------|------|------|
| –COOH 只是加了一个 O | –COOH = 碳 + 双键氧 + 羟基氧，整体是酸基；会给出 H⁺ | 拆开写：C(=O)–OH |
| –NH₂ 是酸 | –NH₂ 是碱；接受 H⁺ 变 –NH₃⁺ | 类比 NH₃（氨气）是碱 |
| 磷酸基只在 DNA 里 | ATP、磷脂头部、信号分子都有 | 画联系图 |
| 疏水 = 不能进细胞 | 疏水分子可以直接穿过磷脂双层！（固醇激素就是） | 接 U2/U4 |

## Part C · FRQ 评分细则

**C1（4 分）**

**(a) Molecule A solubility — 1 分**
- ✅ Predict NO，因为 –CH₂–/–CH₃ 是**非极性**（nonpolar）
- ✅ 说明：非极性分子无法与极性水分子形成氢键 → 不溶
- ❌ 仅说"疏水"不讲原因不得分；必须出现 nonpolar/no hydrogen bonds

**(b) Molecule B partial polarity — 1 分**
- ✅ –COOH 端是极性的（can ionize, can H-bond）
- ✅ 长碳氢链是非极性的（hydrophobic）
- ✅ 两部分共存 → partially polar/partially nonpolar（ampiphilic 也可）

**(c) Molecule C —OH & solubility — 1 分**
- ✅ 识别：–OH (hydroxyl group)
- ✅ 机制：–OH 极性 → 与水分子形成**氢键（hydrogen bonds）** → 溶解
- ❌ 只说"亲水"没有氢键机制不得分

**(d) Adding phosphate to A — 1 分**
- ✅ 溶解性**增加**（becomes more soluble）
- ✅ 原因：–OPO₃²⁻ 带负电荷/极性 → 与极性水分子发生静电吸引/氢键

**满分示范答案**：
> (a) Molecule A would not be soluble in water. Its hydrocarbon chains (–CH₂– and –CH₃) are nonpolar and cannot form hydrogen bonds with water molecules, so water cannot dissolve it.
> (b) The –COOH group at one end is polar (it can ionize and form hydrogen bonds), while the long hydrocarbon chain is nonpolar (hydrophobic). This makes Molecule B partially polar and partially nonpolar.
> (c) The hydroxyl group (–OH) most contributes to solubility. Being polar, –OH can form hydrogen bonds with surrounding water molecules, allowing water to interact with and dissolve Molecule C.
> (d) Solubility would increase. The phosphate group carries negative charges and is highly polar, enabling it to form strong electrostatic interactions and hydrogen bonds with water molecules, pulling the molecule into solution.
