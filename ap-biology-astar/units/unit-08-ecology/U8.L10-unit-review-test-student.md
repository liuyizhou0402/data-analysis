## 📌 Unit 8 大概念地图

```
   个体：对环境响应（行为）L01
        ↓
   种群 Population
   ├ 能量流动（营养级，10%法则）L02
   ├ 增长（指数J / 逻辑斯蒂S，dN/dt，K）L03
   └ 密度依赖/非依赖因子 L04
        ↓
   群落 Community
   ├ 种间关系（竞争/捕食/共生）L05
   ├ 演替/关键种/营养级联 L06
   └ 生物多样性（Simpson，多样性→稳定性）L07
        ↓
   生态系统扰动 L08（入侵种/人类/气候）
```

---

## 一、必记公式/对比

| 项目 | 要点 |
|------|------|
| 指数 vs 逻辑斯蒂 | dN/dt=rN vs rN(K−N)/K |
| 10% 法则 | 每级传~10%，90%散失为热 |
| Simpson | D=1−Σ(n/N)²，越接近1越多样 |
| 密度依赖 vs 非依赖 | 竞争/捕食/病 vs 天气/灾害 |
| 共生 | 互利+/+，偏利+/0，寄生+/− |
| 初生 vs 次生演替 | 无土从零 vs 有土较快 |

---

## 二、易错点总温习

| 知识点 | 正确版本 |
|--------|----------|
| 能量是否循环 | 能量单向流动（不循环），物质循环 |
| dN/dt 公式 | 别漏 (K−N)/K 项 |
| Simpson | 先算n/N→平方→求和→1减 |
| 关键种 | 影响不成比例，非数量多 |
| 入侵种增长 | 初期常指数（无天敌）|

## 三、单元测评 / Unit 8 Assessment

> 建议时间：55 分钟（含增长率 + 多样性计算）

### Section I · MCQ（10 题，每题 1 分）

**1.** Energy transfer between trophic levels averages about
(A) 10%  (B) 90%  (C) 50%  (D) 1%

**2.** Logistic growth produces an S-shaped curve and levels off at
(A) zero  (B) infinity  (C) twice the population  (D) the carrying capacity (K)

**3.** A relationship in which one organism benefits and the other is unharmed is
(A) mutualism  (B) commensalism  (C) parasitism  (D) competition

**4.** Which is a density-independent factor?
(A) Competition  (B) Disease  (C) A hurricane  (D) Predation

**5.** In Simpson's index D = 1 − Σ(n/N)², a higher D means
(A) higher diversity  (B) lower diversity  (C) only one species  (D) no individuals

**6.** Pioneer species such as lichens are characteristic of
(A) climax communities  (B) eutrophication  (C) parasitism  (D) primary succession on bare rock

**7.** A keystone species is one that
(A) is always most abundant  (B) has a disproportionately large effect on its community  (C) is a producer  (D) has no ecological role

**8.** In an ecosystem, energy
(A) is recycled  (B) flows one way and is lost as heat  (C) is created by consumers  (D) increases up the food chain

**9.** An invasive species often grows rapidly at first because it
(A) has many predators  (B) cannot reproduce  (C) is density-limited immediately  (D) lacks natural predators and competitors in the new environment

**10.** Higher biodiversity generally makes an ecosystem
(A) less stable  (B) unable to recover  (C) more stable and resilient  (D) identical to a monoculture

### Section II · FRQ（2 题，共 8 分）

**FRQ 1.** *[Population Growth Calculation｜4 分｜建议 12 分钟]*

A deer population grows logistically with r_max = 0.5 and carrying capacity K = 800.

(a) When N = 200, **calculate** dN/dt. Show your work. *(2)*
(b) **Explain** what happens to the growth rate as the population approaches 800. *(1)*
(c) **Identify** one density-dependent factor that could limit this population near its carrying capacity. *(1)*

**FRQ 2.** *[Biodiversity + Energy｜4 分｜建议 12 分钟]*

(a) A pond has 4 species: A = 40, B = 30, C = 20, D = 10 (total = 100). **Calculate** Simpson's diversity index (D = 1 − Σ(n/N)²). Show your work. *(2)*
(b) **Explain** why an ecosystem can support a greater biomass of producers than of top predators, referring to energy flow. *(2)*

**MCQ 1–10**：A, D, B, C, A, D, B, B, D, C
