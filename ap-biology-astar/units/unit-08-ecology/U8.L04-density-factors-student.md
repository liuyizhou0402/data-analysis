# U8.L04 · Density-Dependent & Independent Factors 密度因素（学生版）

> **本课你将学会 / By the end you can:**
> ① 区分密度依赖因子与密度非依赖因子
> ② 解释它们如何调节种群大小
> ③ 把密度依赖因子连接到逻辑斯蒂增长

---

## 📌 一句话本质 / In One Sentence
> **密度依赖因子（如竞争、疾病）随种群变密而加强，像"负反馈"把种群拉回 K；密度非依赖因子（如天气、灾害）无论种群多密都一样起作用。**

---

## 一、密度依赖因子 / Density-Dependent ⭐

- **定义**：影响强度**随种群密度增加而增强**的因子
- 种群越密 → 这些因子越强 → 抑制增长 → 把种群拉向 K
- **类型**：
  - **资源竞争**（食物、空间、水）
  - **捕食**（密度高→捕食者更易找到猎物）
  - **疾病/寄生**（密度高→传播快）
  - **代谢废物积累**

> 🔁 **串联 L03/U4**：密度依赖因子就是逻辑斯蒂增长里 (K−N)/K 的现实机制——它们像**负反馈**（接 U4.L05），维持种群在 K 附近。

---

## 二、密度非依赖因子 / Density-Independent ⭐

- **定义**：影响强度**与种群密度无关**的因子
- 无论种群多密，影响一样
- **类型**：
  - **气候/天气**（严寒、干旱、洪水）
  - **自然灾害**（火灾、火山、飓风）

> 💡 一场霜冻杀死的昆虫比例，与昆虫种群多密无关——这就是密度非依赖。

| | 密度依赖 | 密度非依赖 |
|---|---|---|
| 随密度变化 | 是（密度高影响大）| 否 |
| 例子 | 竞争、捕食、疾病 | 天气、灾害 |
| 与 K 关系 | 把种群拉向 K | 不直接依赖 K |

---

## 三、综合调节 / Combined Regulation

- 真实种群同时受两类因子影响
- 密度依赖因子提供"反馈调节"使种群趋向 K
- 密度非依赖因子可造成种群骤增骤减（不考虑密度）

## 六、本课练习 / Practice

### Part A · 基础 MCQ

**A1.** A density-dependent factor is one whose effect
(A) is the same regardless of population density  (B) increases as population density increases
(C) only affects small populations  (D) never affects populations

**A2.** Which of the following is a density-independent factor?
(A) Competition for food  (B) A sudden flood  (C) Disease transmission  (D) Predation

**A3.** Disease spreads more rapidly in a crowded population. Disease is therefore a __ factor.
(A) density-independent  (B) density-dependent  (C) abiotic only  (D) carrying capacity

**A4.** Density-dependent factors tend to
(A) push a population toward its carrying capacity  (B) have no effect on population size
(C) only increase population growth  (D) act like positive feedback

**A5.** A harsh winter that kills the same proportion of a beetle population whether the population is large or small is acting as a
(A) density-dependent factor  (B) density-independent factor  (C) carrying capacity  (D) predator

### Part B · 进阶 MCQ

**B1.** As a deer population grows very large, food becomes scarce and starvation increases, slowing population growth. This is an example of
(A) a density-independent factor  (B) a density-dependent factor regulating the population toward K
(C) exponential growth  (D) immigration

**B2.** How are density-dependent factors similar to negative feedback?
(A) They amplify population changes  (B) They counteract growth as density rises, helping stabilize the population near K
(C) They have no effect  (D) They only apply to weather

**B3.** A volcanic eruption destroys a forest, killing organisms regardless of how dense their populations were. This illustrates
(A) a density-dependent factor  (B) competition  (C) a density-independent factor  (D) carrying capacity

### Part C · 真题风格 FRQ

**C1.** *[题型：Conceptual Analysis｜4 分｜建议 10 分钟]*

Populations are regulated by both density-dependent and density-independent factors.

(a) **Distinguish** between density-dependent and density-independent factors, giving an example of each. *(2)*
(b) **Explain** how density-dependent factors help regulate a population around its carrying capacity, comparing this to negative feedback. *(2)*
