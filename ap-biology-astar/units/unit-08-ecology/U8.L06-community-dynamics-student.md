## 📌 一句话本质 / In One Sentence
> **群落不是静止的——它会经历演替（从荒地到森林），而某些"关键种"对整个群落结构有不成比例的巨大影响。**

---

## 一、生态演替 / Ecological Succession ⭐

**演替（succession）**：群落随时间发生的可预测的物种组成变化。

| 类型 | 起点 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| **初生演替 Primary** | 无土壤的裸地（从零开始）| 火山熔岩、退缩冰川后的岩石 |
| **次生演替 Secondary** | 有土壤但植被被清除 | 火灾后、弃耕农田 |

**初生演替过程**：
1. **先锋物种（pioneer species）**：地衣、苔藓（能在裸岩定居，分解岩石成土）
2. 土壤逐渐形成 → 草本 → 灌木 → 乔木
3. 最终达到相对稳定的**顶极群落（climax community）**

> 🔑 次生演替比初生演替**快**，因为已有土壤和种子库。

---

## 二、关键种 / Keystone Species ⭐

- **关键种（keystone species）**：对群落结构有**不成比例的大影响**的物种（相对其数量）
- 移除关键种 → 群落剧变
- 经典例：**海星（Pisaster）**——捕食贻贝，维持潮间带多样性；移除海星 → 贻贝爆发 → 多样性骤降
- 海獭（捕食海胆，保护海藻林）

> 💡 关键种不一定数量多，但其作用（常是捕食或工程）维持整个群落平衡。

---

## 三、营养级联 / Trophic Cascade ⭐

- **营养级联**：某营养级的变化**沿食物链向下传递**，影响多个层级
- 经典例：**黄石公园的狼**
  - 狼回归 → 减少麋鹿 → 河岸植被恢复 → 河狸、鸟类回归 → 河流形态改变
- 顶级捕食者通过控制下层，影响整个生态系统（自上而下调控）

---

## 四、生态系统工程师 / Ecosystem Engineers（拓展）

- 通过改变物理环境影响群落的物种
- 例：河狸筑坝形成湿地；珊瑚建造礁石

## 七、本课练习 / Practice

### Part A · 基础 MCQ

**A1.** Primary succession differs from secondary succession in that primary succession
(A) occurs where soil already exists  (B) begins on bare rock/lifeless areas with no soil
(C) is always faster  (D) does not involve pioneer species

**A2.** The first organisms to colonize a bare area during primary succession are called
(A) climax species  (B) keystone species  (C) pioneer species  (D) decomposers

**A3.** A keystone species is one that
(A) is always the most abundant  (B) has a disproportionately large effect on community structure relative to its abundance
(C) has no effect on the community  (D) is a producer

**A4.** Removing sea stars (a keystone predator) from a tidal zone leads to mussels taking over and a decline in diversity. This shows that the sea star
(A) is unimportant  (B) maintains community diversity through predation
(C) is a pioneer species  (D) causes primary succession

**A5.** The reintroduction of wolves to Yellowstone changed elk behavior, vegetation, and river systems. This is an example of
(A) primary succession  (B) a trophic cascade  (C) commensalism  (D) competitive exclusion

### Part B · 进阶 MCQ

**B1.** Secondary succession (e.g., after a forest fire) generally proceeds faster than primary succession because
(A) there are no pioneer species  (B) soil and seed banks already exist
(C) it starts on bare rock  (D) no organisms are present

**B2.** A predator that controls the population of a dominant herbivore, allowing many plant species to coexist, would best be described as
(A) a pioneer species  (B) a keystone species  (C) a parasite  (D) a decomposer

**B3.** In a trophic cascade, removing a top predator would MOST likely
(A) have no effect on lower trophic levels  (B) cause changes that ripple down through multiple trophic levels
(C) only affect the predator  (D) increase primary succession

### Part C · 真题风格 FRQ

**C1.** *[题型：Conceptual Analysis｜4 分｜建议 10 分钟]*

Communities change over time and are shaped by certain influential species.

(a) **Distinguish** between primary and secondary succession, and **explain** why secondary succession usually proceeds faster. *(2)*
(b) **Explain** what a keystone species is and **predict** what would happen to a community if its keystone predator were removed. *(2)*
