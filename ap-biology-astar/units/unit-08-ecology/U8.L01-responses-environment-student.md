---

## 📌 一句话本质 / In One Sentence
> **生物的行为和生理响应都是"对环境的适应"——能提高生存繁殖（适合度）的响应会被自然选择保留。**

---

## 一、行为类型 / Types of Behavior

| 类型 | 描述 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| **先天行为（innate）** | 遗传决定、无需学习、刻板 | 蜘蛛结网、鸟类筑巢本能、趋性 |
| **习得行为（learned）** | 经经验获得，可改变 | 联想学习、印随、模仿 |

**学习类型**：
- **印随（imprinting）**：关键期形成的固定依恋（如雏鸟跟随第一个移动物体）
- **联想学习（associative learning）**：把刺激与结果联系（如巴甫洛夫条件反射）
- **习惯化（habituation）**：对无害的重复刺激减少反应

---

## 二、趋性与运动 / Taxis & Kinesis

- **趋性（taxis）**：朝向或远离刺激的**定向**移动（如飞蛾趋光 = 正趋光性）
- **运动性（kinesis）**：随机的、非定向的活动改变（如潮虫在干燥处加速移动）

---

## 三、行为如何影响适合度 / Behavior & Fitness ⭐

- **觅食行为**：高效觅食 → 更多能量 → 更高繁殖
- **求偶行为**：求偶展示、信号 → 吸引配偶（接 U7 性选择）
- **协作行为**：群居、利他（如工蜂）→ 提高群体/亲属存活（亲缘选择）
- **迁徙**：躲避恶劣季节、寻找资源

> 🔑 行为是**表现型**，受基因影响，能被自然选择作用（接 U7）——适应性行为提高适合度。

---

## 四、信号与通讯 / Signaling & Communication

- 动物用**信号**通讯：化学（信息素）、视觉（展示）、听觉（鸣叫）、触觉
- 信号协调求偶、警告、领地、群体行为
- 例：蜜蜂摇摆舞传递食物方位；信息素标记路径

> 🔁 串联 U4：细胞和个体层面都用"信号"协调行为——信息传递是大概念。

## 七、本课练习 / Practice

### Part A · 基础 MCQ

**A1.** A behavior that is genetically programmed and performed correctly the first time without learning is
(A) learned behavior  (B) innate behavior  (C) habituation  (D) associative learning

**A2.** A moth flying directly toward a light source exhibits
(A) kinesis  (B) taxis  (C) imprinting  (D) habituation

**A3.** A young duckling following the first moving object it sees after hatching is an example of
(A) associative learning  (B) imprinting  (C) habituation  (D) kinesis

**A4.** Behaviors that increase an organism's ability to survive and reproduce are likely to be
(A) eliminated by natural selection  (B) favored by natural selection
(C) unrelated to fitness  (D) always learned

**A5.** Honeybees performing a "waggle dance" to indicate the location of food is an example of
(A) habituation  (B) communication/signaling  (C) kinesis  (D) imprinting

### Part B · 进阶 MCQ

**B1.** Pill bugs (isopods) move more rapidly and turn more often in dry areas, slowing down in moist areas, eventually accumulating in moist environments. This non-directional response is
(A) taxis  (B) kinesis  (C) imprinting  (D) associative learning

**B2.** A behavior with a genetic basis that improves foraging efficiency would be expected to
(A) decrease in frequency over generations  (B) increase in frequency because individuals with it have higher fitness
(C) have no effect on the population  (D) only occur in plants

**B3.** Why can animal behaviors evolve by natural selection?
(A) Behaviors are never inherited  (B) Behaviors are phenotypes that can have a genetic basis and affect survival and reproduction
(C) Behaviors do not affect fitness  (D) Only physical traits can evolve

### Part C · 真题风格 FRQ

**C1.** *[题型：Conceptual Analysis｜4 分｜建议 10 分钟]*

Animal behaviors can be innate or learned, and both can affect survival and reproduction.

(a) **Distinguish** between innate and learned behavior, giving an example of each. *(2)*
(b) **Explain** how a behavior that improves foraging efficiency could become more common in a population over generations. *(2)*
