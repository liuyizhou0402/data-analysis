> **本课目标**：把减数分裂、遗传规律、卡方、系谱串成一张遗传大网，完成单元测评。

---

## 📌 Unit 5 大概念地图

```
   减数分裂（变异来源）
   ├ 同源分离(减一) + 姐妹单体分离(减二)
   └ 交叉互换 + 自由组合(2ⁿ) + 随机受精
        ↓
   遗传规律（预测）
   ├ 孟德尔：分离 + 自由组合
   ├ 单杂交3:1 / 双杂交9:3:3:1
   ├ 概率：乘法(AND) / 加法(OR)
   └ 卡方检验：观测vs期望是否显著
        ↓
   遗传的复杂性
   ├ 非孟德尔：不完全显性/共显性/复等位/上位/多基因
   ├ 环境×基因型 → 表型
   └ 染色体遗传：伴性、不分离、系谱
```

---

## 一、必记对比表

| 对比 | 关键区别 |
|------|----------|
| 减一 vs 减二 | 同源分离 vs 姐妹单体分离 |
| 减数 vs 有丝 | 4个不同单倍体 vs 2个相同二倍体 |
| 不完全显性 vs 共显性 | 中间型 vs 两者都表达 |
| 乘法 vs 加法法则 | AND相乘 vs OR相加 |
| 卡方 χ²>临界值 | 拒绝H₀（差异显著）|
| 常染色体 vs 伴性 | 性别比例无差异 vs 男性多发(X隐) |

---

## 二、易错点总温习

| 知识点 | 正确版本 |
|--------|----------|
| 卡方 df | = 类别数 − 1 |
| χ² 与临界值 | χ²>临界值 → 拒绝H₀ |
| 不完全显性≠共显性 | 中间型 vs 各自完全表达 |
| 交叉互换位置 | 非姐妹染色单体之间(减一前期) |
| X连锁隐性 | 男性只需1个隐性等位基因即表现 |
| 环境对表型 | 不改变DNA，只影响表达 |

## 三、单元测评 / Unit 5 Assessment

> 建议时间：50 分钟（含卡方计算 FRQ）

### Section I · MCQ（10 题，每题 1 分）

**1.** Homologous chromosomes separate during
(A) meiosis I anaphase  (B) mitosis anaphase  (C) meiosis II anaphase  (D) prophase I

**2.** Meiosis produces
(A) 2 identical diploid cells  (B) 2 haploid cells  (C) 4 genetically different haploid cells  (D) 4 diploid cells

**3.** In a cross Aa × Aa, the expected phenotypic ratio is
(A) 1:1  (B) 9:3:3:1  (C) 1:2:1  (D) 3:1

**4.** The probability of an aabb offspring from AaBb × AaBb is
(A) 9/16  (B) 1/16  (C) 1/4  (D) 3/16

**5.** Degrees of freedom for a chi-square test with 2 categories is
(A) 1  (B) 2  (C) 0  (D) 3

**6.** Pink snapdragons from red × white parents demonstrate
(A) codominance  (B) epistasis  (C) incomplete dominance  (D) sex linkage

**7.** A person with type AB blood demonstrates
(A) codominance  (B) incomplete dominance  (C) sex linkage only  (D) epistasis

**8.** X-linked recessive disorders are more common in males because males
(A) have two X chromosomes  (B) inherit their X from their father  (C) cannot be carriers  (D) have one X, so one recessive allele is expressed

**9.** Down syndrome (trisomy 21) results from
(A) crossing over  (B) nondisjunction during meiosis  (C) incomplete dominance  (D) a point mutation

**10.** Siamese cat coat color varying with body temperature shows that
(A) the environment changes the genotype  (B) coat color is codominant
(C) phenotype is affected by both genotype and environment  (D) crossing over occurred

### Section II · FRQ（2 题，共 8 分）

**FRQ 1.** *[Short FRQ — Chi-Square｜4 分｜建议 12 分钟]*

A dihybrid cross is expected to produce a 9:3:3:1 ratio. A student counts 320 offspring: 170 round-yellow, 65 round-green, 60 wrinkled-yellow, 25 wrinkled-green.

(a) **Calculate** the expected number for each phenotype assuming a 9:3:3:1 ratio. *(1)*
(b) **State** the null hypothesis and **identify** the degrees of freedom. *(1)*
(c) The chi-square value calculated is 1.65, and the critical value (df=3, p=0.05) is 7.81. **Determine** whether to accept or reject the null hypothesis and **explain** what this means. *(2)*

**FRQ 2.** *[Pedigree / Sex-linkage｜4 分｜建议 12 分钟]*

Color blindness is X-linked recessive. A carrier woman (X^C X^c) marries a man with normal vision (X^C Y).

(a) **Draw** a Punnett square showing the possible offspring genotypes. *(1)*
(b) **Determine** the probability that a son is color blind and the probability that a daughter is color blind. *(2)*
(c) **Explain** why none of the daughters will be color blind in this particular cross. *(1)*
