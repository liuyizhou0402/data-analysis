# U5.L07 · Environmental Effects on Phenotype 环境对表型的影响（学生版）

> **本课你将学会 / By the end you can:**
> ① 解释表型由基因型与环境共同决定
> ② 举例说明环境如何改变表型（不改变基因型）
> ③ 理解表型可塑性的概念

---

## 📌 一句话本质 / In One Sentence
> **基因型给出"潜力范围"，环境决定"实际表现"——表型 = 基因型 + 环境。** 同样的基因，在不同环境下可以长成不同模样。

---

## 一、表型 = 基因型 + 环境 / Phenotype = Genotype + Environment

- **基因型**设定了可能的范围（norm of reaction，反应规范）
- **环境**（温度、营养、光照、pH 等）决定在范围内的实际表型
- 环境**不改变基因型/DNA 序列**，只影响基因的表达和表型

---

## 二、经典案例 / Classic Examples ⭐

| 案例 | 环境因素 | 效果 |
|------|----------|------|
| **喜马拉雅兔/暹罗猫毛色** | 温度 | 体温低的部位（耳、爪、尾）酶活跃 → 深色；体温高的躯干 → 浅色 |
| **绣球花花色** | 土壤 pH | 酸性土→蓝花；碱性土→粉花（同一植株）|
| **水毛茛叶形** | 水上/水下 | 水下叶细裂，水上叶宽阔（同一基因型）|
| **人类身高/肤色** | 营养、日晒 | 营养好→更高；日晒→肤色加深 |

> 💡 暹罗猫的"重点色"是温度敏感酶的经典例：产生色素的酶在低温（四肢末端）才有活性，所以耳朵、爪子、尾巴是深色。

---

## 三、表型可塑性 / Phenotypic Plasticity

- **定义**：同一基因型在不同环境下产生不同表型的能力
- 是一种**适应**——让生物应对多变环境（接 U7、U8）
- 例：植物在阴处长更大的叶（捕光）；同一基因型在不同环境表现不同

---

## 四、多基因 + 环境 = 连续变异

- 多基因性状（L06）+ 环境影响 → 表型呈**连续分布**（钟形曲线）
- 例：人类身高由多个基因 + 营养共同决定 → 平滑的连续范围

---

- ⬇ 下游：U6 基因表达调控（环境如何影响表达）；U7 表型可塑性与适应；U8 环境响应
- 🌐 大概念：**基因与环境的相互作用**

---

## 六、考点提示 / Exam Tips

- 🎯 **必考**：解释环境如何影响表型而不改变基因型。
- 🎯 暹罗猫/喜马拉雅兔（温度敏感酶）、绣球花（pH）等经典例。
- 🎯 表型可塑性的适应意义。
- ⚠️ 环境**不改变 DNA 序列**——它影响表达和表型（区别于突变）。

## 七、本课练习 / Practice

### Part A · 基础 MCQ

**A1.** The phenotype of an organism is determined by
(A) genotype only  (B) environment only  (C) both genotype and environment  (D) neither

**A2.** Himalayan rabbits and Siamese cats have dark fur on their extremities (ears, paws, tail) because
(A) those areas have different genes  (B) a temperature-sensitive enzyme produces pigment only in cooler regions
(C) the environment changes their DNA  (D) of crossing over

**A3.** Hydrangea flowers are blue in acidic soil and pink in basic soil, even on the same plant. This demonstrates that
(A) flower color is controlled by codominance  (B) the environment (soil pH) influences phenotype
(C) the plant has multiple genotypes  (D) crossing over occurred

**A4.** Environmental effects on phenotype
(A) change the organism's DNA sequence  (B) do not change the genotype but affect how it is expressed
(C) only occur in animals  (D) eliminate genetic variation

**A5.** The ability of one genotype to produce different phenotypes in different environments is called
(A) codominance  (B) phenotypic plasticity  (C) epistasis  (D) independent assortment

### Part B · 进阶 MCQ

**B1.** Two genetically identical plant clones are grown, one in full sun and one in shade. The shade plant grows larger, thinner leaves. This difference is BEST explained by
(A) a mutation in the shade plant  (B) phenotypic plasticity—the same genotype responding to different environments
(C) different genotypes  (D) codominance

**B2.** Why does a temperature-sensitive coat color (as in Siamese cats) NOT change the cat's genotype?
(A) Because temperature changes the DNA sequence  (B) Because the environment affects enzyme activity and gene expression, not the underlying DNA
(C) Because all cats have the same genotype  (D) Because coat color is not inherited

### Part C · 真题风格 FRQ

**C1.** *[题型：Conceptual Analysis｜3 分｜建议 8 分钟]*

Phenotype results from the interaction of genotype and environment.

(a) **Explain**, using the example of temperature-sensitive fur color in Siamese cats, how the environment can affect phenotype without changing the genotype. *(2)*
(b) **Predict** what would happen to the fur color pattern of a Siamese cat raised in a very warm environment, and **justify** your answer. *(1)*
