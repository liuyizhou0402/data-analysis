## 二、单元测评 / Unit 4 Assessment

> 建议时间：45 分钟

### Section I · MCQ（10 题，每题 1 分）

**1.** A steroid hormone (hydrophobic) most likely binds to
(A) a membrane receptor  (B) an intracellular receptor  (C) DNA polymerase  (D) a carbohydrate

**2.** The main advantage of a multi-step phosphorylation cascade is
(A) slowing the response  (B) amplifying the signal  (C) using less ATP  (D) preventing any response

**3.** Insulin lowering elevated blood glucose is an example of
(A) positive feedback  (B) negative feedback  (C) signal amplification  (D) an antagonist

**4.** DNA is replicated during which phase?
(A) G1  (B) S  (C) G2  (D) M (anaphase)

**5.** Sister chromatids separate during
(A) prophase  (B) metaphase  (C) anaphase  (D) telophase

**6.** Cyclin-dependent kinases (CDKs) are active only when bound to
(A) glucose  (B) cyclins  (C) DNA  (D) oxygen

**7.** A mutation converting a proto-oncogene into an oncogene causes the gene to be
(A) inactive  (B) overactive, promoting excessive division  (C) a tumor suppressor  (D) a checkpoint

**8.** The tumor suppressor protein p53 normally
(A) promotes uncontrolled division  (B) detects DNA damage and triggers repair or apoptosis
(C) acts as an oncogene  (D) builds the spindle

**9.** Uterine contractions during childbirth intensifying via oxytocin is an example of
(A) negative feedback  (B) positive feedback  (C) an antagonist  (D) a checkpoint

**10.** A drug that binds a receptor and blocks the natural ligand without activating it is
(A) an agonist  (B) an antagonist  (C) a second messenger  (D) a kinase

### Section II · FRQ（2 题，共 8 分）

**FRQ 1.** *[Analyze Model｜4 分｜建议 10 分钟]*

A pathway controls cell division: **Growth factor → Receptor → Kinase cascade → Transcription of division genes → Cell division**.

(a) **Explain** how the kinase cascade amplifies the growth factor signal. *(1)*
(b) **Predict** and **explain** the effect on cell division if the receptor becomes permanently active due to a mutation. *(2)*
(c) **Explain** how the loss of a checkpoint protein (e.g., p53) could combine with this mutation to cause cancer. *(1)*

**FRQ 2.** *[Conceptual Analysis — Feedback｜4 分｜建议 10 分钟]*

(a) **Distinguish** between negative and positive feedback. *(1)*
(b) **Explain** how negative feedback regulates blood glucose after a meal, including the role of insulin. *(2)*
(c) **Identify** whether blood clotting (platelets recruiting more platelets) is positive or negative feedback, and **justify**. *(1)*
