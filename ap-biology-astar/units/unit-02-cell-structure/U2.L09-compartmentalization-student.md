---

## 📌 一句话本质 / In One Sentence
> **区室化（compartmentalization）= 把细胞分成专门的"房间"，让不同的化学反应在各自最优条件下并行进行，互不干扰，大幅提升效率。**

---

## 一、区室化的优势 / Why Compartmentalization Helps

膜把真核细胞内部分隔成不同区室（细胞器），带来三大优势：

1. **局部环境最优化**：每个细胞器维持自己最适的 pH、离子、酶（如溶酶体 pH 5、线粒体基质特定环境）
2. **反应物浓缩**：把酶和底物集中在小空间 → 提高反应速率
3. **互不干扰**：把可能冲突的反应隔离（如溶酶体的水解酶不会消化整个细胞）

> 🔗 串联 L02/L03：溶酶体酸性环境（L02）、线粒体嵴增大表面积（L01/L03）——都是区室化提效的体现。

> 💡 **类比**：餐厅厨房分区（备菜区、炒菜区、洗碗区）→ 各区专门化、并行工作、效率高。如果全挤在一个台面，必然混乱。

---

## 二、内共生学说 / Endosymbiotic Theory ⭐

**核心主张**：线粒体和叶绿体起源于**被早期真核细胞吞入但未被消化的自由生活的原核生物**，二者形成互利共生关系，逐渐演化为细胞器。

- 线粒体 ← 来自能进行有氧呼吸的好氧细菌
- 叶绿体 ← 来自能进行光合作用的蓝细菌（cyanobacteria）

---

## 三、支持证据 / Evidence ⭐（FRQ 高频）

线粒体和叶绿体拥有与原核细胞相似的特征：

| 证据 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| **自己的环状 DNA** | 像细菌一样有独立的环状 DNA（不同于核 DNA） |
| **自己的核糖体（70S）** | 与细菌核糖体大小相同，不同于真核 80S |
| **双层膜** | 外膜来自宿主吞噬时的膜，内膜来自原核生物自身 |
| **自我复制（二分裂）** | 像细菌一样通过二分裂独立增殖 |
| **大小与细菌相似** | 直径约 1–10 μm |

> 🎯 **考试要求**：能列出至少 3 条证据，并解释为什么这些证据支持"它们曾是独立的原核生物"。

> 🔁 串联 U7：内共生是进化（共同祖先、协同演化）的重要例证；70S 核糖体的保守性也是分子进化证据（接 U7.L7）。

## 六、本课练习 / Practice

### Part A · 基础 MCQ

**A1.** Compartmentalization in eukaryotic cells increases efficiency primarily because
(A) it reduces the number of organelles needed  (B) it allows incompatible reactions to occur in separate optimized environments
(C) it eliminates the need for membranes  (D) it slows down all chemical reactions

**A2.** Which of the following is evidence for the endosymbiotic theory?
(A) Mitochondria and chloroplasts have a single membrane  (B) Mitochondria and chloroplasts contain their own circular DNA and 70S ribosomes
(C) Mitochondria are found only in plant cells  (D) Chloroplasts cannot reproduce on their own

**A3.** According to the endosymbiotic theory, mitochondria most likely evolved from
(A) viruses  (B) free-living aerobic bacteria engulfed by an early eukaryotic cell
(C) the nucleus  (D) the endoplasmic reticulum

**A4.** The fact that lysosomes maintain an internal pH of ~5 while the cytoplasm is ~7 is an example of
(A) active transport failure  (B) compartmentalization allowing different conditions in different organelles
(C) osmosis  (D) endocytosis

**A5.** Mitochondria and chloroplasts reproduce by a process resembling
(A) mitosis of the whole cell  (B) binary fission, like bacteria
(C) meiosis  (D) exocytosis

### Part B · 进阶 MCQ

**B1.** Which observation would MOST strengthen the endosymbiotic theory?
(A) Mitochondrial DNA sequences are more similar to certain bacteria than to the cell's nuclear DNA
(B) Mitochondria are smaller than the nucleus
(C) Chloroplasts are green  (D) Both organelles are found inside cells

**B2.** A researcher finds that the ribosomes inside chloroplasts are inhibited by antibiotics that block bacterial (70S) ribosomes but NOT by drugs that block eukaryotic (80S) ribosomes. This finding
(A) contradicts the endosymbiotic theory  (B) supports the endosymbiotic theory, since chloroplast ribosomes resemble bacterial ones
(C) shows chloroplasts have no ribosomes  (D) proves chloroplasts came from the nucleus
