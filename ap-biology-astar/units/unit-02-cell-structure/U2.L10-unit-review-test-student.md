## 📌 Unit 2 大概念地图

```
【细胞 = 区室化的系统】
        |
        ▼
   细胞器（专门车间）
   ├ 核/核糖体（信息→蛋白）
   ├ 内膜系统（分泌流水线：核糖体→ER→高尔基→囊泡→胞吐）
   ├ 线粒体/叶绿体（能量；内共生起源）
   └ 区室化 → 局部最优 → 效率
        |
        ▼
   细胞膜（流动镶嵌）
   └ 疏水核心 → 选择透过性
        |
        ▼
   膜运输
   ├ 被动（顺梯度，无ATP）：简单扩散 / 易化扩散 / 渗透
   ├ 主动（逆梯度，耗ATP）：钠钾泵 / 共运输
   └ 大宗：胞吞 / 胞吐
        |
        ▼
   稳态（水势 Ψ=Ψp+Ψs 调控水分平衡）
```

## 一、本单元必记对比表

| 对比 | 关键区别 |
|------|----------|
| 被动 vs 主动运输 | 顺梯度无ATP vs 逆梯度耗ATP |
| 简单 vs 易化扩散 | 直接穿膜（不饱和）vs 借蛋白（饱和） |
| 通道 vs 载体蛋白 | 孔道 vs 构象改变翻转 |
| 低渗/等渗/高渗 | 外部溶质 少/等/多；水 进/平/出 |
| 动物 vs 植物细胞（低渗） | 裂解 vs 膨压挺立（有细胞壁） |
| 线粒体 vs 叶绿体 | 呼吸（嵴）vs 光合（类囊体）；都内共生起源 |

---

## 二、易错点总温习

| 知识点 | 正确版本 |
|--------|----------|
| 易化扩散是否耗能 | **不耗能**（顺梯度，只是借蛋白） |
| "用了蛋白就是主动运输" | 错！判断标准是**梯度方向 + ATP** |
| 低渗 = 水少 | 错！低渗 = **溶质少** = 水势高 |
| Ψs 正负 | Ψs **总是 ≤ 0**；纯水 Ψ=0 |
| 水势计算温度单位 | **开尔文**（°C+273） |
| i 值 | 蔗糖=1，NaCl=2，CaCl₂=3 |

## 三、单元测评 / Unit 2 Assessment

> 建议时间：50 分钟（MCQ 20 分钟 + FRQ 30 分钟）

### Section I · MCQ（10 题，每题 1 分）

**1.** As a cell grows larger, which change creates a challenge for material exchange?
(A) Surface area increases faster than volume  (B) Volume increases faster than surface area, decreasing SA:V
(C) The membrane becomes impermeable  (D) The nucleus disappears

**2.** A protein is synthesized for secretion. Which sequence correctly traces its path?
(A) Smooth ER → nucleus → Golgi → membrane  (B) Rough ER → Golgi → vesicle → plasma membrane
(C) Golgi → rough ER → lysosome → membrane  (D) Ribosome → mitochondria → Golgi → vesicle

**3.** Which molecule crosses the phospholipid bilayer most easily without a transport protein?
(A) Glucose  (B) Na⁺  (C) CO₂  (D) A nucleic acid

**4.** Facilitated diffusion of glucose into a cell
(A) requires ATP because it uses a protein  (B) moves glucose against its gradient
(C) moves glucose down its gradient without ATP  (D) only occurs in plant cells

**5.** A red blood cell placed in distilled (pure) water will most likely
(A) shrink due to water loss  (B) remain unchanged  (C) swell and possibly lyse  (D) become turgid

**6.** The sodium-potassium pump is an example of active transport because it
(A) moves ions down their gradients  (B) moves Na⁺ and K⁺ against their gradients using ATP
(C) requires no energy  (D) uses simple diffusion

**7.** Which is evidence supporting the endosymbiotic theory?
(A) Mitochondria have ribosomes identical to the cell's cytoplasmic ribosomes
(B) Mitochondria and chloroplasts have their own circular DNA and 70S ribosomes
(C) Both organelles lack membranes  (D) Both are found only in animals

**8.** A solution has a solute potential (Ψs) of −3.0 bar and is in an open container. Its total water potential (Ψ) is
(A) +3.0 bar  (B) 0 bar  (C) −3.0 bar  (D) −6.0 bar

**9.** Compartmentalization benefits eukaryotic cells by
(A) preventing all chemical reactions  (B) allowing specialized conditions for different reactions in separate organelles
(C) reducing the cell's surface area  (D) eliminating the need for the membrane

**10.** Cells lining the small intestine have many microvilli (finger-like projections). These increase the rate of nutrient absorption by
(A) decreasing the surface area  (B) increasing the surface-area-to-volume ratio for absorption
(C) producing more ATP  (D) storing more glucose

### Section II · FRQ（2 题，共 8 分）

**FRQ 1.** *[Short FRQ — Calculation & Analysis｜4 分｜建议 12 分钟]*

A plant cell with an internal water potential of Ψ = −7.0 bar is placed in an open beaker containing a 0.3 M sucrose solution at 25°C.

(a) **Calculate** the solute potential (Ψs) of the sucrose solution. (i = 1, R = 0.0831 L·bar/mol·K). Show your work. *(2)*

(b) **Determine** the water potential (Ψ) of the sucrose solution and **predict** the direction of net water movement (into or out of the cell). **Justify** your answer. *(2)*

**FRQ 2.** *[Short FRQ — Conceptual Analysis｜4 分｜建议 12 分钟]*

(a) **Distinguish** between passive transport and active transport in terms of energy use and direction of movement relative to the concentration gradient. *(2)*

(b) A cell must take up amino acids from its surroundings, where amino acid concentration is LOWER than inside the cell. **Identify** the type of transport required and **explain** why ATP is necessary. *(2)*
