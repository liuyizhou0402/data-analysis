## 📌 Unit 7 大概念地图

```
   变异（突变U6 + 重组U5）
        ↓
   自然选择（三要素：变异+遗传+差异繁殖）
   ├ 选择类型：定向/稳定/分裂
   └ 人工选择（同机制）
        ↓
   群体遗传 + Hardy-Weinberg
   ├ 进化=等位基因频率改变
   ├ p+q=1；p²+2pq+q²=1
   ├ 5条件/5力量（选择/漂变/基因流/突变/非随机交配）
   └ 偏离HW = 进化在发生
        ↓
   宏观进化
   ├ 证据：化石/解剖(同源)/分子(保守序列)
   ├ 共同祖先 + 系统发育树(共有衍征)
   ├ 物种形成(生殖隔离：异域/同域)
   └ 灭绝 / 多样性 / 生命起源(RNA world)
```

## 一、必记对比/公式

| 项目 | 要点 |
|------|------|
| 自然选择三条件 | 变异+遗传+差异繁殖 |
| HW 公式 | p+q=1；p²+2pq+q²=1；q²=隐性表现型 |
| 5 进化力量 | 选择/漂变/基因流/突变/非随机交配 |
| 同源 vs 同功 | 共同祖先 vs 趋同进化 |
| 异域 vs 同域 | 有地理隔离 vs 无（多倍体）|
| 合子前 vs 合子后 | 阻止受精 vs 杂种有问题 |
| cladogram 亲缘 | 看最近共同祖先节点 |

---

## 二、易错点总温习

| 知识点 | 正确版本 |
|--------|----------|
| 谁进化 | 群体进化，个体不进化 |
| HW 偏离 | 偏离=进化在发生 |
| q 求法 | q=√q²（先开方）|
| 适合度 | =繁殖成功率，非体力 |
| 抗药性 | 变异先存在，药物只筛选 |
| cladogram | 看节点不看末端左右 |

## 三、单元测评 / Unit 7 Assessment

> 建议时间：60 分钟（含 HW 计算 + cladogram 长题）

### Section I · MCQ（10 题，每题 1 分）

**1.** Evolution is best defined as
(A) a change in allele frequencies in a population over generations  (B) a change in an individual's traits during life
(C) the survival of the largest organisms  (D) the appearance of identical offspring

**2.** Which is required for natural selection?
(A) Identical individuals  (B) No competition
(C) Heritable variation and differential reproduction  (D) Organisms changing their DNA on purpose

**3.** In a Hardy-Weinberg population, q² represents the frequency of
(A) the recessive allele  (B) heterozygotes  (C) the dominant allele  (D) homozygous recessive individuals

**4.** Homologous structures (e.g., the forelimbs of mammals) provide evidence of
(A) common ancestry  (B) convergent evolution  (C) artificial selection  (D) genetic drift

**5.** Selection that favors the average phenotype and reduces variation is
(A) directional  (B) stabilizing  (C) disruptive  (D) sexual

**6.** A geographic barrier splits a population, which then diverges into two species. This is
(A) sympatric speciation  (B) convergent evolution  (C) genetic drift  (D) allopatric speciation

**7.** In a cladogram, two species are most closely related if they
(A) are listed next to each other  (B) have the most differences
(C) share the most recent common ancestor  (D) are on the longest branch

**8.** Antibiotic resistance evolves because
(A) pre-existing resistant bacteria survive and reproduce  (B) antibiotics create resistance mutations
(C) bacteria decide to resist  (D) all bacteria are identical

**9.** A small population's allele frequencies change randomly due to chance. This is
(A) natural selection  (B) gene flow  (C) mutation  (D) genetic drift

**10.** The Miller-Urey experiment showed that
(A) cells form spontaneously  (B) oxygen was abundant early
(C) organic molecules can form under early-Earth conditions  (D) DNA was the first molecule

### Section II · FRQ（2 题，共 12 分）

**FRQ 1.** *[Hardy-Weinberg Calculation｜6 分｜建议 18 分钟]*

In a population of 1000 beetles, body color is controlled by one gene. Black (B) is dominant to brown (b). 90 beetles are brown (bb). Assume Hardy-Weinberg equilibrium.

(a) **Calculate** the frequency of the recessive allele (q) and the dominant allele (p). Show your work. *(2)*
(b) **Calculate** the expected number of heterozygous (Bb) beetles. *(2)*
(c) A predator begins preferentially eating brown beetles. **Predict** how the allele frequencies will change over time and **identify** the evolutionary force responsible. *(2)*

**FRQ 2.** *[Cladogram Analysis｜6 分｜建议 18 分钟]*

The character table shows traits for four species (✓ = present), with a sea star as the outgroup.

| Species | Backbone | Four limbs | Amniotic egg |
|---|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Sea star (outgroup) | | | |
| Shark | ✓ | | |
| Frog | ✓ | ✓ | |
| Turtle | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |

(a) **Determine** which two species are most closely related and **justify** using shared derived characters. *(2)*
(b) **Explain** the role of the outgroup in constructing this cladogram. *(2)*
(c) **Explain** how molecular (DNA sequence) data could be used to test the relationships shown in this cladogram. *(2)*
