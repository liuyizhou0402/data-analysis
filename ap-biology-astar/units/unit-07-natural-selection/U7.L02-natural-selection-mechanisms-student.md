# U7.L02 · Natural Selection Mechanisms 自然选择机制（学生版）

> ① 区分定向选择、稳定选择、分裂选择
> ② 区分自然选择的不同类型对性状分布的影响

---

## 📌 一句话本质 / In One Sentence
> **自然选择有三种"塑形"方式——定向（偏向一端）、稳定（偏向中间）、分裂（偏向两端）——它们以不同方式改变群体的性状分布。**

---

## 一、三种选择类型 / Three Types ⭐

> 用性状分布的钟形曲线（接 U5 多基因连续变异）来理解：

### 1. 定向选择 Directional Selection
- 偏向**一个极端** → 分布曲线**整体移向一端**
- 例：抗生素抗性（抗性强的存活）、长颈鹿颈长
- 环境变化时常见

### 2. 稳定选择 Stabilizing Selection
- 偏向**中间型**，淘汰两个极端 → 分布**变窄**（峰更高）
- 例：人类出生体重（太大太小都风险高，中等最优）

### 3. 分裂选择 Disruptive Selection
- 偏向**两个极端**，淘汰中间 → 分布变成**双峰**
- 例：某些鸟喙大小（大喙吃大种子、小喙吃小种子，中等无优势）
- 可能导致物种分化（接 L10）

| 类型 | 偏好 | 曲线变化 | 例子 |
|------|------|----------|------|
| 定向 | 一端 | 整体移动 | 抗药性 |
| 稳定 | 中间 | 变窄 | 出生体重 |
| 分裂 | 两端 | 双峰 | 鸟喙大小 |

---

## 二、性选择 / Sexual Selection

- 基于**获得配偶**的选择压力
- **类型**：
  - 同性竞争（如雄鹿争斗）
  - 异性选择（如雌孔雀选择尾巴华丽的雄孔雀）
- 可能导致**性二态（sexual dimorphism）**：雌雄外观差异
- 有时产生看似"不利生存"的性状（如华丽尾巴），但有利繁殖

---

## 三、其他选择相关概念 / Other Concepts

- **平衡选择（balancing selection）**：维持多个等位基因（如镰刀细胞杂合者抗疟疾——杂合优势）
- **选择压力（selective pressure）**：环境施加的、影响存活繁殖的因素
- **适应（adaptation）**：经选择产生的、提高适合度的性状

## 六、本课练习 / Practice

### Part A · 基础 MCQ

**A1.** Selection that favors one extreme phenotype, shifting the population's trait distribution in one direction, is
(A) stabilizing selection  (B) directional selection  (C) disruptive selection  (D) sexual selection

**A2.** Human birth weight, where very small and very large babies have lower survival, is an example of
(A) directional selection  (B) stabilizing selection  (C) disruptive selection  (D) artificial selection

**A3.** Disruptive selection favors
(A) the average phenotype  (B) one extreme only  (C) both extreme phenotypes over the intermediate  (D) no phenotypes

**A4.** A peacock's large, colorful tail, which attracts mates but may hinder escape from predators, is best explained by
(A) stabilizing selection  (B) sexual selection  (C) disruptive selection  (D) genetic drift

**A5.** The heterozygote advantage in sickle cell trait (resistance to malaria) is an example of
(A) directional selection only  (B) balancing selection maintaining both alleles
(C) disruptive selection  (D) no selection

### Part B · 进阶 MCQ

**B1.** After a new predator arrives that easily catches slow individuals, a prey population's average running speed increases over generations. This is an example of
(A) stabilizing selection  (B) directional selection  (C) disruptive selection  (D) sexual selection

**B2.** On an island, medium-sized seeds become rare, leaving only large and small seeds. Over time, birds with large beaks and birds with small beaks both thrive, but medium-beaked birds decline. This illustrates
(A) stabilizing selection  (B) directional selection  (C) disruptive selection  (D) artificial selection

**B3.** Which type of selection would tend to REDUCE genetic variation in a population the most, narrowing the distribution around the average?
(A) Directional  (B) Disruptive  (C) Stabilizing  (D) Sexual

### Part C · 真题风格 FRQ

**C1.** *[题型：Analyze Data / Visual Representation｜4 分｜建议 10 分钟]*

A population's trait distribution (a bell curve) changes over several generations.

(a) **Describe** how the trait distribution would change under directional selection versus stabilizing selection. *(2)*
(b) A population of finches experiences a drought where only very large and very small seeds remain. **Predict** the type of selection that would result and **describe** its effect on beak-size distribution. *(2)*
