# U7.L01 · Introduction to Natural Selection 自然选择导论（学生版）

> **本课你将学会 / By the end you can:**
> ① 陈述达尔文自然选择的核心逻辑
> ② 识别自然选择的三个必要条件
> ③ 理解"适者生存"的真正含义

---

## 📌 一句话本质 / In One Sentence
> **自然选择 = 有变异 + 变异可遗传 + 不同变异导致不同繁殖成功率 → 有利变异在群体中越来越多。** 这就是进化的引擎。

---

## 一、达尔文的核心观察与推论 / Darwin's Logic ⭐

**观察**：
1. 生物产生的后代远多于能存活的（过度繁殖）
2. 个体之间存在**变异（variation）**
3. 资源有限 → **生存竞争（struggle for existence）**

**推论**：
- 拥有**有利变异**的个体更可能存活和繁殖
- 这些有利变异**遗传**给后代
- 经过许多世代，有利变异在群体中**积累** → 群体逐渐适应环境（进化）

---

## 二、自然选择的三个必要条件 / Three Requirements ⭐

> 缺一不可，是判断"能否发生自然选择"的金标准。

| 条件 | 含义 |
|------|------|
| **变异（Variation）** | 群体中个体性状有差异 |
| **遗传（Heritability）** | 这些性状可以遗传给后代 |
| **差异繁殖（Differential reproduction）** | 不同性状导致不同的存活/繁殖成功率 |

> 🔑 三者齐备 → 有利等位基因频率上升 → 进化。

---

## 三、"适者生存"的真正含义 / What "Fitness" Really Means

- **适合度（fitness）**不是"最强壮"，而是**繁殖成功率**——能留下最多可育后代的个体
- "适者"= 最适应当前环境、繁殖最成功的个体（不一定是体格最强的）

> ⚠️ **高频误区**：
> - 自然选择作用于**表现型（phenotype）**，但改变的是**等位基因频率**（群体层面）
> - 个体**不进化**，**群体**才进化
> - 生物**不是为了适应而主动改变**——变异是随机的，选择是非随机的

---

## 四、变异的来源 / Sources of Variation

- **突变（mutation）**：终极来源（接 U6.L09）
- **有性生殖**：交叉互换、自由组合、随机受精（接 U5.L02）
- 选择**不创造**变异，只是**筛选**已有变异

---

## 五、知识串联 / Connections

- ⬆ 上游：U5.L02 遗传多样性；U6.L09 突变（变异来源）
- ⬇ 下游：L02 选择类型；L04–L06 群体遗传与 HW（量化）；L07 证据
- 🌐 大概念：**进化**——统一解释生命

---

## 六、考点提示 / Exam Tips

- 🎯 **必考**：自然选择三条件（变异、遗传、差异繁殖）。
- 🎯 适合度=繁殖成功率，不是体力。
- 🎯 群体进化、个体不进化；选择作用于表现型。
- ⚠️ 变异是随机产生的，选择是非随机的——生物不会"主动为需要而进化"。

## 七、本课练习 / Practice

### Part A · 基础 MCQ

**A1.** Which of the following is NOT required for natural selection to occur?
(A) Variation among individuals  (B) Heritability of traits
(C) Differential reproductive success  (D) Individuals changing their own DNA to adapt

**A2.** In evolutionary terms, "fitness" refers to an organism's
(A) physical strength  (B) reproductive success  (C) body size  (D) lifespan only

**A3.** Natural selection acts directly on an organism's __, while changing the __ of a population over time.
(A) genotype; phenotype  (B) phenotype; allele frequencies  (C) DNA; ribosomes  (D) proteins; chromosomes

**A4.** The ultimate source of the genetic variation that natural selection acts on is
(A) mutation  (B) the environment  (C) natural selection itself  (D) reproduction without variation

**A5.** Which statement about natural selection is correct?
(A) Individuals evolve during their lifetimes  (B) Populations evolve over generations
(C) Organisms mutate on purpose when needed  (D) Selection creates new variation

### Part B · 进阶 MCQ

**B1.** A population of beetles has varied body colors. In a dark forest, darker beetles survive predation better and reproduce more. Over generations, the population becomes darker. This requires that body color is
(A) not heritable  (B) heritable, so the advantageous trait is passed to offspring
(C) caused by the environment only  (D) the same in all beetles

**B2.** Which scenario would PREVENT natural selection from occurring in a population?
(A) All individuals are genetically identical (no variation)  (B) Some individuals reproduce more than others
(C) Traits are heritable  (D) There is competition for resources

**B3.** A student says, "Giraffes developed long necks because they stretched to reach high leaves, and passed the longer necks to their offspring." The BEST scientific correction is
(A) the statement is correct  (B) variation in neck length already existed; those with longer necks reproduced more, increasing the frequency of that trait
(C) giraffes chose to evolve  (D) the environment changed the giraffes' DNA directly

### Part C · 真题风格 FRQ

**C1.** *[题型：Conceptual Analysis + Argumentation｜4 分｜建议 10 分钟]*

A population of bacteria is exposed to an antibiotic. Most bacteria die, but a few survive and reproduce, and soon the whole population is resistant.

(a) **Identify** the three conditions necessary for natural selection and **explain** how each applies to this example. *(3)*
(b) **Explain** why it is incorrect to say the bacteria "became resistant because they needed to." *(1)*
