---

## 📌 一句话本质 / In One Sentence
> **进化不是"假说"，而是由化石、解剖、胚胎、分子等多条独立证据链共同支持的科学理论——它们都指向"共同祖先 + 随时间变化"。**

---

## 一、五类证据 / Five Lines of Evidence ⭐

| 证据 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| **化石（fossil）** | 记录了生物随时间的变化、过渡型（如鱼→两栖、恐龙→鸟）|
| **解剖（anatomical）** | 同源结构、痕迹器官 |
| **胚胎（embryological）** | 不同脊椎动物早期胚胎相似（如咽囊）|
| **分子（molecular）** | DNA/蛋白质序列相似度反映亲缘关系（最有力）|
| **生物地理（biogeography）** | 物种分布反映演化历史（如岛屿特有种）|

---

## 二、同源 vs 同功结构 / Homologous vs Analogous ⭐

| | 同源结构（homologous）| 同功结构（analogous）|
|---|---|---|
| 定义 | 结构相似（同源），功能可不同 | 功能相似，结构来源不同 |
| 反映 | **共同祖先（common ancestry）** | **趋同进化（convergent evolution）** |
| 例子 | 人臂、鲸鳍、蝙蝠翼、猫腿（五指骨骼布局相同）| 鸟翼 vs 昆虫翼（都飞但结构不同）|

> 🔑 **同源结构**支持共同祖先（结构相同因为继承自同一祖先）；**同功结构**是不同祖先因相似环境压力独立进化出相似功能（趋同）。

- **痕迹器官（vestigial structures）**：失去原功能的退化结构（如人类阑尾、蟒蛇的残余后肢骨）→ 支持"由有功能祖先演化而来"

---

## 三、分子证据为何最有力 / Why Molecular Evidence ⭐

- 所有生物用**相同的遗传密码**（接 U6.L05）→ 支持共同祖先
- DNA/蛋白质序列**相似度**越高 → 亲缘关系越近
- **保守序列（conserved sequences）**：重要基因（如核糖体 rRNA、细胞色素 c）在差异极大的生物间仍相似 → 来自共同祖先
- 可量化 → 构建系统发育树（接 L09）

> 🔁 串联 U6：通用遗传密码、保守的代谢通路（糖酵解）都是分子层面的共同祖先证据。

## 五、考点提示 / Exam Tips

- 🎯 **必考**：同源 vs 同功结构（共同祖先 vs 趋同进化）。
- 🎯 分子证据（序列相似度、保守序列、通用密码）。
- 🎯 痕迹器官的意义。
- ⚠️ 同源=结构相同（共同祖先）；同功=功能相同但结构异（趋同）。

## 六、本课练习 / Practice

### Part A · 基础 MCQ

**A1.** The forelimbs of humans, whales, bats, and cats have the same basic bone arrangement but different functions. These are
(A) analogous structures  (B) homologous structures  (C) vestigial structures  (D) convergent structures

**A2.** Homologous structures provide evidence of
(A) convergent evolution  (B) common ancestry  (C) artificial selection  (D) genetic drift

**A3.** The wings of a bird and the wings of an insect, which have different underlying structures but similar functions, are
(A) homologous  (B) analogous (result of convergent evolution)  (C) vestigial  (D) identical

**A4.** Which type of evidence is generally considered the MOST powerful for establishing evolutionary relationships?
(A) Fossil size  (B) DNA and protein sequence comparisons  (C) Body color  (D) Habitat only

**A5.** The human appendix and the pelvic bones of whales are examples of
(A) analogous structures  (B) vestigial structures  (C) homologous functions  (D) convergent evolution

### Part B · 进阶 MCQ

**B1.** Two species share 98% of their DNA sequence, while a third species shares only 70% with them. The BEST conclusion is that
(A) the third species is more closely related  (B) the two species sharing 98% are more closely related (more recent common ancestor)
(C) DNA cannot show relationships  (D) all three are equally related

**B2.** The fact that nearly all organisms use the same genetic code (codons specify the same amino acids) is evidence for
(A) independent origins of each species  (B) a shared common ancestor for all life
(C) convergent evolution  (D) artificial selection

**B3.** Dolphins (mammals) and sharks (fish) have similar streamlined body shapes but evolved them independently. This is an example of
(A) homologous structures from common ancestry  (B) convergent evolution producing analogous structures
(C) vestigial structures  (D) genetic drift

### Part C · 真题风格 FRQ

**C1.** *[题型：Conceptual Analysis + Argumentation｜4 分｜建议 10 分钟]*

Multiple independent lines of evidence support the theory of evolution.

(a) **Distinguish** between homologous and analogous structures, and **explain** what each reveals about evolutionary relationships. *(2)*
(b) **Explain** why molecular evidence (DNA/protein sequences) is considered strong support for common ancestry. *(2)*
