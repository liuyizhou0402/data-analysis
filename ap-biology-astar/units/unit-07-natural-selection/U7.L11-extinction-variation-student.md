# U7.L11 · Extinction & Variation in Populations 灭绝与群体变异（学生版）

> **本课你将学会 / By the end you can:**
> ① 解释灭绝的原因及其在进化中的角色
> ② 解释遗传多样性对群体存续的重要性
> ③ 理解变异如何影响群体对环境变化的适应能力

---

## 📌 一句话本质 / In One Sentence
> **灭绝是进化的常态（>99% 物种已灭绝），而一个群体的遗传多样性越高，越可能在环境剧变中"赌中"能存活的变异。**

---

## 一、灭绝 / Extinction

- **灭绝（extinction）**：一个物种完全消失
- 地球历史上 **>99%** 曾存在的物种已灭绝——灭绝是常态
- **背景灭绝**：持续的、低速率的灭绝
- **大灭绝（mass extinction）**：短期内大量物种灭绝（如白垩纪末恐龙灭绝）

**原因**：
- 环境剧变（气候、海平面）
- 灾难事件（小行星撞击、火山）
- 人类活动（栖息地破坏、过度捕猎、入侵种——接 U8）

**灭绝的进化意义**：
- 释放生态位 → 幸存类群**适应辐射（adaptive radiation）**（如恐龙灭绝后哺乳动物繁盛）

---

## 二、遗传多样性的重要性 / Why Diversity Matters ⭐

> 这是本课核心考点，也串联整个单元。

- **遗传多样性高** = 群体中有更多不同等位基因/性状
- 当环境改变时，多样性高的群体**更可能**有个体携带能适应新环境的变异 → 存活繁殖
- **遗传多样性低** = 群体脆弱：
  - 一种疾病/环境变化可能消灭整个群体（缺乏抗性变异）
  - 近交（inbreeding）增加有害隐性等位基因纯合 → 适合度下降

> 🔑 **核心逻辑**：变异是自然选择的"原材料"——没有变异，就没有可供选择的对象，群体无法适应变化。

---

## 三、案例 / Examples

- **猎豹**：曾经历瓶颈效应（接 L04），遗传多样性极低 → 对疾病敏感、繁殖力下降 → 易危
- **单一栽培作物**：基因高度一致 → 一种病害可摧毁整片（如爱尔兰马铃薯饥荒）
- 保护生物学强调**维持遗传多样性**以保护濒危物种

## 六、本课练习 / Practice

### Part A · 基础 MCQ

**A1.** Approximately what percentage of species that have ever existed are now extinct?
(A) 10%  (B) 50%  (C) 75%  (D) over 99%

**A2.** A population with HIGH genetic diversity is generally
(A) more likely to go extinct  (B) better able to survive environmental changes because some individuals may have advantageous variations
(C) identical in all traits  (D) unable to evolve

**A3.** After a mass extinction, surviving groups often diversify rapidly to fill empty niches. This is called
(A) genetic drift  (B) adaptive radiation  (C) artificial selection  (D) gene flow

**A4.** Why is low genetic diversity a problem for a population facing a new disease?
(A) It guarantees survival  (B) The population may lack individuals with resistance, so the disease could wipe it out
(C) It increases variation  (D) It has no effect

**A5.** Cheetahs have very low genetic diversity due to a past population bottleneck. This makes them
(A) highly resistant to disease  (B) more vulnerable to disease and reproductive problems
(C) more genetically variable  (D) immune to extinction

### Part B · 进阶 MCQ

**B1.** A farmer plants a single genetically identical (clonal) crop variety across a huge area. The GREATEST risk of this practice is that
(A) the crop will have too much variation  (B) a single pest or disease could destroy the entire crop because all plants share the same vulnerabilities
(C) the crop cannot be harvested  (D) the plants will evolve too quickly

**B2.** Two populations of the same species face a sudden cold snap. Population A has high genetic diversity; Population B has low diversity. Which is MORE likely to survive, and why?
(A) Population B, because it is uniform  (B) Population A, because it is more likely to contain individuals with cold-tolerance variations
(C) Both equally  (D) Neither can survive

**B3.** How does extinction contribute to the overall process of evolution?
(A) It stops evolution  (B) It opens ecological niches that surviving lineages can adapt to and diversify into
(C) It reduces all variation permanently  (D) It prevents speciation

### Part C · 真题风格 FRQ

**C1.** *[题型：Argumentation｜4 分｜建议 10 分钟]*

Genetic diversity is important for the long-term survival of populations.

(a) **Explain** why a population with greater genetic diversity is more likely to survive a significant environmental change. *(2)*
(b) **Explain** why a population that has experienced a severe bottleneck (like the cheetah) may be at increased risk of extinction. *(2)*
