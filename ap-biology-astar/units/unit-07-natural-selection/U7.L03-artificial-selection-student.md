# U7.L03 · Artificial Selection 人工选择（学生版）

---

## 📌 一句话本质 / In One Sentence
> **人工选择 = 人类充当"选择者"，挑选有想要性状的个体繁殖——它和自然选择机制相同，只是选择压力来自人而非环境。**

---

## 一、人工选择 / Artificial Selection

- **定义**：人类有意识地选择具有**理想性状**的个体进行繁殖
- 例：
  - 农作物（高产玉米、大果番茄）
  - 家畜（产奶多的奶牛、瘦肉型猪）
  - 宠物（各种犬种都源于狼）
- 经过许多世代，理想性状在群体中频率上升

> 💡 达尔文用人工选择（如鸽子育种）作为类比，论证自然选择的可能性。

---

## 二、人工选择 vs 自然选择 / Comparison ⭐

| | 人工选择 | 自然选择 |
|---|---|---|
| 选择者 | **人类** | **环境** |
| 选择标准 | 人类想要的性状（可能对生存不利！）| 提高生存/繁殖的性状 |
| 速度 | 通常较快 | 通常较慢 |
| 机制 | **相同**：差异繁殖改变等位基因频率 | 相同 |
| 例子 | 犬种、作物 | 抗药性、拟态 |

> 🔑 **核心共性**：两者都通过**差异繁殖**改变群体的等位基因频率——这是进化的统一机制。

> ⚠️ 人工选择的性状可能**降低**野外生存力（如某些纯种犬的健康问题），因为人选的是"人想要的"而非"利于生存的"。

---

## 三、作为进化证据 / Evidence for Evolution

- 人工选择**在短时间内**就能显著改变物种（如几十年培育出新作物品种）
- 证明：选择压力 + 可遗传变异 → 群体可快速改变
- 支持自然选择在更长时间尺度上同样能产生巨大变化（接 L07 证据）

> 🔁 **Lab 1（人工选择）**：常用快速繁殖的生物（如威斯康星快速植物 Wisconsin Fast Plants）演示几代内的性状选择。

## 六、本课练习 / Practice

### Part A · 基础 MCQ

**A1.** Artificial selection differs from natural selection mainly in that
(A) it uses a different genetic mechanism  (B) humans, rather than the environment, choose which individuals reproduce
(C) it does not change allele frequencies  (D) it cannot produce new breeds

**A2.** All domestic dog breeds descended from wolves through
(A) natural selection only  (B) artificial selection by humans  (C) mutation alone  (D) genetic drift

**A3.** A trait favored by artificial selection
(A) always improves survival in the wild  (B) may actually reduce survival in the wild because humans select for desired traits
(C) cannot be inherited  (D) never changes the population

**A4.** Both artificial and natural selection change a population by
(A) creating new mutations  (B) altering allele frequencies through differential reproduction
(C) preventing reproduction  (D) keeping the population identical

**A5.** Darwin used artificial selection (e.g., pigeon breeding) to
(A) disprove evolution  (B) illustrate how selection could change species over time
(C) show that traits are not heritable  (D) demonstrate genetic drift

### Part B · 进阶 MCQ

**B1.** A farmer breeds only the cows that produce the most milk. After many generations, average milk production rises substantially. This demonstrates that
(A) milk production is not heritable  (B) selecting which individuals reproduce can change a population's traits over generations
(C) the environment changed the cows' DNA  (D) milk production cannot be selected

**B2.** Why can artificial selection serve as evidence supporting natural selection?
(A) It shows selection cannot change organisms  (B) It demonstrates that selection on heritable variation can produce significant changes in populations, just on a human-directed and faster timescale
(C) It proves organisms evolve on purpose  (D) It eliminates variation

### Part C · 真题风格 FRQ

**C1.** *[题型：Conceptual Analysis｜4 分｜建议 10 分钟]*

Humans have produced hundreds of dog breeds from a wolf ancestor through artificial selection.

(a) **Explain** how artificial selection produces breeds with specific traits, referring to heritable variation and differential reproduction. *(2)*
(b) **Compare** artificial selection and natural selection in terms of who/what does the selecting and the underlying genetic mechanism. *(2)*
