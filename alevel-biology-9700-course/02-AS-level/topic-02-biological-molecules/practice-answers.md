| 5 | **C** | β-1,4 糖苷键→直链→**氢键形成微纤维→高抗拉强度**。A 描述淀粉（螺旋）；B 描述糖原（支链）；D 错误。 |
| 6 | **C** | n 个氨基酸缩合形成 **(n-1)** 个肽键，释放 (n-1) 个水分子。80 个氨基酸→释放 **79** 个水分子。 |

## Part B — 结构题（按采分点 ✓ 拆解）

### B1
**(a) Two properties of water related to hydrogen bonds. [2]** — *State（给两个事实）*
任两条各 1 分：
- ✓ High **specific heat capacity**（吸收大量热才升温小，稳定温度）
- ✓ High **latent heat of vaporisation**（蒸发带走大量热，降温效果好）
- ✓ **Cohesion**（水分子间氢键产生内聚力）/ surface tension
- ✓ Good **solvent**（极性使其溶解离子和极性分子）

**(b) Explain why water is polar. [2]** — *Explain（讲原因）*
- ✓ Oxygen is more **electronegative** than hydrogen. **[1]**
- ✓ Electrons are drawn towards O, giving O a slight negative charge (δ–) and H a slight positive charge (δ+) → overall **dipole / uneven charge distribution**. **[1]**

**(c) Importance of high specific heat capacity. [2]** — *Explain*
- ✓ Large amount of heat energy needed to raise temperature of water **[1]**
- ✓ → temperature of aquatic environment stays relatively **stable / does not fluctuate widely**, protecting organisms from rapid temperature changes. **[1]**

### B2
**(a) Name the bond between monomers. [1]** — *Name*
- ✓ **Glycosidic bond** **[1]**

**(b) Type of reaction. [1]** — *State*
- ✓ **Condensation** (reaction) **[1]**

**(c) Test for starch + expected result. [2]** — *Describe*
- ✓ Add **iodine solution** (iodine dissolved in potassium iodide). **[1]**
- ✓ Positive result: solution turns **blue-black**. **[1]**
- ⚠️ 说"碘液变蓝"不够准确，需说 **blue-black**；负结果为 yellow-brown/orange。

**(d) Compare amylose and cellulose. [4]** — *Compare（必须成对）*
任四组，每组成对：
- ✓ Amylose has **α-1,4 glycosidic bonds** whereas cellulose has **β-1,4 glycosidic bonds**.
- ✓ Amylose forms a **helical (coiled) chain** whereas cellulose forms **straight / unbranched chains**.
- ✓ Amylose chains are held together by relatively few hydrogen bonds; cellulose chains **hydrogen-bond to adjacent chains** forming **microfibrils**.
- ✓ Amylose is a **storage** polysaccharide whereas cellulose provides **structural support** (cell wall).
- ⚠️ compare 必须成对；只写一方不给分。

### B3
**(a) Draw and label general structure of amino acid. [3]** — *Draw / Label*
- ✓ Central carbon with **–NH₂** (amino group) labelled. **[1]**
- ✓ **–COOH** (carboxyl group) labelled. **[1]**
- ✓ **–R** (variable/R group) and **–H** labelled. **[1]**

**(b) Primary structure. [2]** — *Explain*
- ✓ The **sequence of amino acids** in a polypeptide chain. **[1]**
- ✓ Held together by **peptide bonds**. **[1]**

**(c) Bonds maintaining tertiary structure. [4]** — *Describe*
- ✓ **Hydrogen bonds** between –R groups. **[1]**
- ✓ **Ionic bonds** between oppositely charged –R groups. **[1]**
- ✓ **Disulfide bonds** between –SH groups of cysteine residues (covalent). **[1]**
- ✓ **Hydrophobic interactions** between non-polar –R groups (fold away from water). **[1]**
- ⚠️ 漏写疏水相互作用（hydrophobic interactions）是最常见丢分点——很多学生只写三种。

### B4
**(a) Structure of triglyceride. [3]** — *Describe*
- ✓ **One glycerol** molecule bonded to **three fatty acid** chains. **[1]**
- ✓ Joined by **ester bonds** (formed by condensation, releasing water). **[1]**
- ✓ Fatty acids may be **saturated** (no C=C double bonds) or **unsaturated** (one or more C=C). **[1]**

**(b) Compare triglyceride and phospholipid. [3]** — *Compare（成对）*
- ✓ Triglyceride has **three** fatty acid tails; phospholipid has **two** fatty acids and a **phosphate group**.
- ✓ Triglyceride is entirely **hydrophobic**; phospholipid has a **hydrophilic phosphate head** and **hydrophobic fatty acid tails** (amphipathic).
- ✓ Triglyceride functions mainly in **energy storage**; phospholipid forms **cell membranes (bilayers)**.

**(c) Emulsion test + expected result. [2]** — *Describe*
- ✓ Mix sample with **ethanol**, shake, then add to **water**. **[1]**
- ✓ Positive result: a **white/milky emulsion** forms. **[1]**

### B5
**(a) Quaternary structure. [2]** — *Define*
- ✓ Protein consisting of **more than one** polypeptide chain (subunit). **[1]**
- ✓ Subunits held together by **same bonds as tertiary structure** (H-bonds, ionic, disulfide, hydrophobic). **[1]**

**(b) Compare globular and fibrous proteins. [4]** — *Compare（成对）*
- ✓ Globular proteins are **spherical / folded** into compact shapes; fibrous proteins are **elongated / rope-like**.
- ✓ Globular proteins are generally **soluble** in water; fibrous proteins are generally **insoluble**.
- ✓ Globular proteins have **metabolic functions** (enzymes, hormones, antibodies); fibrous proteins have **structural roles** (collagen, keratin).
- ✓ Globular proteins have **variable / complex tertiary structure**; fibrous proteins have **regular, repetitive** secondary structure.

## Part C — 应用/数据题

### C1
**(a) Identify sample containing starch but no reducing sugar. [1]** — *Identify*
- ✓ **Sample B** (Iodine → blue-black ✓; Benedict's → blue = no reducing sugar). **[1]**

**(b) Identify sample that is most likely pure protein. [1]** — *Identify*
- ✓ **Sample C** (Biuret → purple ✓; no starch; no reducing sugar; no lipid). **[1]**

**(c) Suggest what sample D contains. [3]** — *Suggest（迁移，言之有理即可）*
- ✓ D contains **reducing sugar** (Benedict's → brick red). **[1]**
- ✓ D contains **lipid** (emulsion test → white emulsion). **[1]**
- ✓ D does **not** contain protein (Biuret → blue) or starch (iodine → yellow-brown). **[1]**

**(d) Why sample A gives brick-red despite containing lipid. [2]** — *Explain*
- ✓ Benedict's test detects **reducing sugar**, which is also present in sample A. **[1]**
- ✓ Lipids do **not interfere** with the Benedict's reaction — the two tests are independent. **[1]**

### C2
**(a) Calculate number of amino acids. [3]** — *Calculate（写步骤）*
- ✓ Number of peptide bonds = (relative mol. mass of protein) / (average M_r of amino acid) — adjust for water released. **[1]**
  Simple approach: approximate amino acids = 11000 / 110 = **100 amino acids**. ✓ **[1]**
  (More precise: each aa has M_r ≈ 110; each peptide bond releases H₂O (M_r 18); for n aa: M_r ≈ 110n − 18(n−1) = 92n + 18 → 92n = 10982 → n ≈ **119 amino acids**) ✓ **[1]**
  Accept either approach with working shown.

**(b) Peptide bonds in each subunit. [2]** — *Calculate*
- ✓ Each subunit has half the amino acids: 100/2 = 50 (or 119/2 ≈ 60) aa per subunit. **[1]**
- ✓ Peptide bonds = number of aa − 1 = **49** (or 59). **[1]**

**(c) Suggest why actual may be higher. [1]** — *Suggest*
- ✓ Some amino acids have a **higher** relative molecular mass than average (the M_r of 110 is a mean). **[1]**

## Part D — 实验技能

### D1
**(a) Procedure to test for non-reducing sugar. [4]** — *Outline（按步骤）*
- ✓ 1. Test original solution with **Benedict's reagent** (negative result → no reducing sugar). **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Add **dilute HCl** (or other acid) and **heat** to hydrolyse the glycosidic bond. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. **Neutralise** with sodium hydrogencarbonate (NaHCO₃) — Benedict's only works at alkaline pH. **[1]**
- ✓ 4. Re-test with Benedict's reagent and heat — positive result (brick-red) indicates non-reducing sugar was present. **[1]**
- ⚠️ 遗漏"中和步骤"是最高频失分：Benedict's 在酸性环境中不起作用，所以**必须中和**后再测。

**(b) Safety precaution with HCl. [1]** — *State*
- ✓ Wear **eye protection / goggles**; or work in **fume cupboard**; or add acid carefully **away from eyes**. **[1]**

**(c) Conclusion and limitation. [2]** — *Evaluate*
- ✓ Conclusion: the solution contains a **non-reducing disaccharide or polysaccharide** (e.g. sucrose). **[1]**
- ✓ Limitation: the acid hydrolysis is not specific — cannot distinguish between different non-reducing sugars without further testing. **[1]**

## 老师讲评建议 / Marking tips
1. **多糖结构-功能只说一半**：Compare 题最常见错误是只写一种多糖的特征，不对应写另一种。强调"compare 必须成对"。
2. **三级结构漏写疏水相互作用**：学生通常能写出氢键/离子键/二硫键，但漏掉第四种疏水相互作用，直接丢 1 分。
3. **非还原糖检测遗漏中和步骤**：这是实验题经典陷阱，每年都有。每节课结束都要强调。
