## Part B — 结构题（按采分点 ✓ 拆解）

### B1
**(a) Cardiac cycle electrical sequence. [4]** — *Describe（按顺序）*
- ✓ 1. SAN in the **right atrium wall** generates the electrical impulse. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Impulse spreads across both **atria**, causing them to contract (atrial systole). **[1]**
- ✓ 3. Impulse reaches **AVN**, which introduces a **short delay**. **[1]**
- ✓ 4. Impulse passes down the **Bundle of His / Purkinje fibres** to the ventricle walls, causing **ventricular systole** from the apex upward. **[1]**

**(b) Why left ventricle has thicker wall. [2]** — *Explain*
- ✓ 1. The left ventricle pumps blood into the **systemic circulation**, which is longer / at higher resistance. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Requires **greater pressure** → thicker muscular wall to generate it. **[1]**

**(c) Role of chordae tendinae. [1]** — *State*
- ✓ Prevent the **atrioventricular valves from inverting / being pushed back** into the atria during ventricular contraction. **[1]**

### B2
**(a) When AV valve opens, with reasoning. [2]** — *Identify + Explain*
- ✓ 1. AV valve opens when **ventricular pressure falls below atrial pressure** (at the start of ventricular diastole). **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Atrial pressure exceeds ventricular pressure → valve is pushed open, blood flows into ventricle. **[1]**

**(b) What causes aortic valve to open. [2]** — *Explain*
- ✓ 1. During ventricular systole, **ventricular pressure rises**. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. When ventricular pressure exceeds **aortic pressure**, the semilunar valve is pushed open. **[1]**

**(c) Calculate heart rate. [2]** — *Calculate*
- ✓ 1. Heart rate = 60 ÷ 0.8. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. = **75 beats per minute**. **[1]**

### B3
**(a) Structure of haemoglobin. [3]** — *Describe*
- ✓ 1. **Four polypeptide chains** (2α and 2β subunits). **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Each chain has one **haem group** containing **Fe²⁺**. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. Each Fe²⁺ can combine with **one O₂** → maximum 4 O₂ per Hb molecule. **[1]**

**(b) Why S-shaped dissociation curve. [2]** — *Explain*
- ✓ 1. The binding of the first O₂ causes a **conformational change** in Hb. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. This makes subsequent O₂ molecules bind **more easily (cooperative binding)** — resulting in a steep S-shaped curve. **[1]**

**(c) Bohr effect and physiological significance. [4]** — *Explain*
- ✓ 1. CO₂ produced in respiring tissues dissolves in plasma → forms carbonic acid → **lowers pH (increases H⁺)**. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. H⁺ binds to Hb, altering its shape → **reduces Hb's affinity for O₂**. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. The oxygen-haemoglobin dissociation curve **shifts to the right**. **[1]**
- ✓ 4. This means Hb releases **more O₂ at lower pO₂** in respiring tissues → more O₂ available for aerobic respiration. **[1]**
- ⚠️ 易错："右移 = 亲和力增加"——完全相反。右移 = 亲和力**降低** = 释放更多 O₂。

### B4
**(a) How tissue fluid forms at arterial end. [3]** — *Explain*
- ✓ 1. Blood has a **high hydrostatic pressure** (due to heart pumping) at the arterial end of the capillary. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. This **exceeds the osmotic pressure** exerted by plasma proteins. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. Fluid (water, small molecules) is **forced out of the capillary by filtration** into the interstitial space. **[1]**

**(b) Why plasma proteins stay in blood. [1]**
- ✓ Plasma proteins are **too large** to pass through the capillary wall. **[1]**

**(c) Damaged thoracic duct → leg swelling. [2]** — *Suggest*
- ✓ 1. Thoracic duct drains lymph back into the venous system; if damaged, lymph **cannot return** to circulation. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Excess **tissue fluid / lymph accumulates** in legs → oedema / swelling. **[1]**

## Part C — 应用/数据题

### C1
**(a) Additional O₂ delivered due to Bohr effect. [2]** — *Calculate*
- ✓ 1. At pH 7.2, saturation is 50%; at pH 7.4, saturation is 70%. Additional O₂ released = 70 − 50 = 20 percentage points. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Percentage increase = 20 / 70 × 100 = **28.6%** more O₂ delivered. **[1]**

**(b) Biochemical mechanism of curve shift. [3]** — *Explain*
- ✓ 1. Exercise increases aerobic (and anaerobic) respiration → **more CO₂ produced**. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. CO₂ + H₂O → H₂CO₃ → **H⁺ + HCO₃⁻**; pH falls from 7.4 to 7.2. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. H⁺ binds to Hb → changes quaternary structure → **Hb affinity for O₂ decreases** → curve shifts right. **[1]**

**(c) Lactic acid effect on curve. [2]** — *Predict + Explain*
- ✓ 1. Lactic acid further **lowers pH** below 7.2, so curve would shift **further to the right** of pH 7.2 curve. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. More H⁺ ions bind to Hb → further reduction in affinity → even more O₂ released. **[1]**

---

### C2
**(a) Difference in plasma protein concentration. [2]** — *Describe + Explain（引用数据）*
- ✓ 1. Plasma protein concentration much higher in blood plasma (0.85) than tissue fluid (0.02 mmol dm⁻³). **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Plasma proteins are **too large to pass** through capillary walls, so they are retained in the blood. **[1]**

**(b) Oxygen and CO₂ concentrations difference. [3]** — *Explain*
- ✓ 1. O₂ is higher in plasma (8.5) than tissue fluid (3.1) — O₂ **diffuses from blood into respiring cells** down a concentration gradient. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. CO₂ is higher in tissue fluid (2.1) than plasma (1.4) — CO₂ is **produced by cell respiration** and diffuses into blood. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. This maintains the **diffusion gradients** for gas exchange between blood and tissues. **[1]**

**(c) Slightly lower glucose in tissue fluid. [1]** — *Suggest*
- ✓ Cells are **actively using glucose** for respiration, so glucose concentration in tissue fluid is slightly reduced as it moves into cells. **[1]**

## Part D — 实验技能

### D1
**(a) Reduce random error in pulse measurement. [2]** — *Describe*
- ✓ 1. **Repeat measurements** (count multiple 1-minute intervals) and calculate a mean. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Ensure the subject is at rest for a **fixed time before measuring** (standardise conditions). **[1]**

**(b) Two reasons heart rate increases during exercise. [2]** — *Suggest*
- ✓ Any two: Muscles need **more ATP → more O₂ → more CO₂ produced** → chemoreceptors detect change → **sympathetic nervous system** accelerates SAN ／ adrenaline released → **increases SAN firing rate** ／ more CO₂ / lower pH detected by medullary centres. **[2]**

**(c) Ethical concern. [1]** — *Suggest*
- ✓ Any one: Risk of **cardiovascular strain / harm** to subject during intense exercise ／ subject must give **informed consent** ／ need medical screening to exclude high-risk individuals. **[1]**

## 老师讲评建议 / Marking tips
- B3(c) 玻尔效应：学生常丢失"机制链"中的某一步——要从 CO₂→pH→H⁺→Hb 构象→亲和力，完整四步。
- B4(a) 组织液形成：明确说"static hydrostatic pressure exceeds osmotic pressure"，不能只说"血液渗出"。
- C1(a) 计算：注意题目给的是两条曲线在同一 pO₂ 的饱和度差值，不是相对百分比——先用差值，再视题意决定是否算百分比。
- Q5/Q6 是全班共同失分点：玻尔效应右移 = 亲和力**降低**；HbF 曲线**左**侧 = 亲和力**更高**。用"左高右低"口诀帮助记忆。
