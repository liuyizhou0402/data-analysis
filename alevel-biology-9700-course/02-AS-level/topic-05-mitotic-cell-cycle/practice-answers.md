## Part B — 结构题（按采分点 ✓ 拆解）

### B1
**(a) S phase. [1]** — *State*
- ✓ **DNA replication** (semi-conservative). **[1]**

**(b) Calculate mitotic index. [2]** — *Calculate*
- ✓ Total cell cycle = 10+8+4+2 = 24 h; time in M phase = 2 h. **[1]**
- ✓ MI = 2/24 = **0.083** (accept 1/12 or ~8%). **[1]**

**(c) Why DNA replication must precede mitosis. [2]** — *Explain*
- ✓ So that each daughter cell receives a **complete / full set** of DNA / chromosomes. **[1]**
- ✓ Without replication, daughter cells would have **only half** the DNA → cell function impaired. **[1]**

### B2
**(a) Identify stage for P, Q, R, S. [4]** — *Identify*
- ✓ P = **Prophase** (chromosomes condensed, nuclear envelope absent). **[1]**
- ✓ Q = **Metaphase** (chromosomes aligned at equator). **[1]**
- ✓ R = **Anaphase** (chromatids moving to poles). **[1]**
- ✓ S = **Telophase** (new nuclear envelopes forming). **[1]**

**(b) Importance of cell Q (metaphase). [2]** — *Explain*
- ✓ Chromosomes aligned at the **equatorial plate** ensures spindle fibres from **opposite poles** attach to each sister chromatid. **[1]**
- ✓ This ensures that when chromatids separate in anaphase, **equal and complete sets** of chromosomes go to each pole. **[1]**

### B3
**(a) Define mitotic index. [1]** — *Define*
- ✓ The proportion of cells **undergoing mitosis** (in M phase) out of the total cells observed: MI = (cells in mitosis) / (total cells). **[1]**

**(b) Suggest what MI values indicate. [2]** — *Suggest*
- ✓ Normal intestinal cells (MI = 0.06): only **6%** of cells are dividing at any time → controlled, relatively slow division. **[1]**
- ✓ Cancerous cells (MI = 0.28): **28%** of cells dividing → cell cycle regulation is **disrupted / uncontrolled**, cells divide more rapidly. **[1]**

**(c) How cancerous cells differ in cell cycle regulation. [3]** — *Explain*
- ✓ **Mutations** in proto-oncogenes or tumour suppressor genes. **[1]**
- ✓ Cell cycle **checkpoints fail** to halt division when DNA is damaged or conditions not appropriate. **[1]**
- ✓ Cells **divide continuously / without limit** → form a tumour. **[1]**

### B4
**(a) Two ways mitosis is important. [2]** — *State*
- ✓ **Growth** of the organism (increase in cell number). **[1]**
- ✓ **Repair and replacement** of damaged or worn-out cells. **[1]**
  (Also accept: asexual reproduction)

**(b) Three differences between mitosis and meiosis. [3]** — *Compare（成对）*
- ✓ Mitosis produces **2 daughter cells**; meiosis produces **4**. **[1]**
- ✓ Mitosis produces cells **genetically identical** to the parent; meiosis produces **genetically varied** cells. **[1]**
- ✓ Mitosis does **not** reduce chromosome number; meiosis results in **haploid** cells (chromosome number halved). **[1]**

## Part C — 数据题

### C1
**(a) Mitotic index for control. [2]** — *Calculate*
- ✓ Cells in mitosis = 23+11+6+5 = 45; total = 200. **[1]**
- ✓ MI = 45/200 = **0.225**. **[1]**

**(b) Describe and explain difference in metaphase counts. [3]** — *Describe + Explain*
- ✓ After colchicine treatment, metaphase count **increased** from 11 to **89**. **[1]** ← Describe + 引数据
- ✓ Colchicine **prevents spindle formation / inhibits microtubule polymerisation**. **[1]**
- ✓ Chromosomes reach the equatorial plate but **cannot be pulled to poles** → cells are **arrested at metaphase**. **[1]**

**(c) Why zero cells in anaphase and telophase. [2]** — *Explain*
- ✓ Anaphase requires **spindle fibres** to pull chromatids apart; without spindle, anaphase cannot occur. **[1]**
- ✓ Since anaphase does not occur, telophase (which follows) also cannot occur. **[1]**

**(d) Suggest why total cell number increased. [2]** — *Suggest*
- ✓ Cells continue to enter and progress through early mitosis but **cannot complete division** (no spindle). **[1]**
- ✓ New cells entering mitosis accumulate; cells that normally exited mitosis cannot → **accumulation** increases total count. **[1]**

### C2
**(a) Describe relationship between smoking and lung cancer. [3]** — *Describe + 引数据*
- ✓ There is a **positive correlation** between cigarette consumption and lung cancer incidence. **[1]**
- ✓ Lung cancer incidence **follows** cigarette consumption with a lag of approximately **20–25 years**. **[1]**
- ✓ Both variables **increased steeply** between approximately 1920 and 1960 (引用时间段). **[1]**

**(b) Explain the time lag. [2]** — *Explain*
- ✓ Cancer develops due to **accumulation of mutations** in DNA over time (not immediate). **[1]**
- ✓ Many cell divisions must occur with mutated DNA before a tumour develops — this takes years to decades. **[1]**

**(c) Strengthen evidence for causation. [2]** — *Suggest*
- ✓ Carry out **longitudinal studies** (follow groups of smokers and non-smokers over decades) to show the relative risk. **[1]**
- ✓ Identify the **specific carcinogens** in tobacco smoke and show they cause DNA mutations leading to lung cancer (mechanistic evidence). **[1]**

## Part D — 实验技能

### D1
**(a) Why hydrochloric acid. [1]** — *Explain*
- ✓ HCl **softens / breaks down** the cell wall and middle lamella (pectin), allowing cells to be **squashed apart** so individual cells can be observed. **[1]**

**(b) Purpose of stain + cellular component. [2]**
- ✓ Purpose: **to make chromosomes (DNA) visible** under light microscope. **[1]**
- ✓ It stains **chromosomes / DNA** (chromatin). **[1]**

**(c) Safety precaution + reason. [2]**
- ✓ Wear **gloves** / avoid skin contact. **[1]**
- ✓ Aceto-orcein contains **ethanoic acid (acetic acid)** which is corrosive and can cause skin/eye irritation. **[1]**

**(d) Calculate actual diameter. [2]** — *Calculate*
- ✓ A = I/M; I = 0.06 mm = 60 μm; M = 400. **[1]**
- ✓ A = 60 / 400 = **0.15 μm**. **[1]**
- ⚠️ 注意单位换算：题目给 mm，答案要 μm → 先换算再除。

## 老师讲评建议 / Marking tips
1. **PMAT 事件描述混淆**：学生常把 prophase（染色体凝缩、纺锤体形成）与 anaphase（着丝粒分裂、染色单体分离）混淆，或把 telophase 的核膜重建归到 metaphase。建议用"PMAT 事件表"让学生填充对应事件。
2. **数据题不引用具体数值**：C1a 计算题和 C2a 描述题都要求明确数字，不引用表格/图中数据直接丢分。
3. **癌症 Explain 链条不完整**：标准链条是：突变（mutation）→ 细胞周期检查点失控（checkpoint failure）→ 不受控分裂（uncontrolled division）→ 肿瘤（tumour）→ 恶性肿瘤转移（metastasis）。只说"突变导致癌症"不足。
