## Part B — 结构题（按采分点 ✓ 拆解）

### B1
**(a) Describe phagocytosis. [4]** — *Describe（步骤清晰）*
- ✓ 1. Phagocyte is **attracted to the pathogen** by chemical signals (chemotaxis). **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Phagocyte extends **pseudopodia** to engulf the pathogen → forms a **phagosome**. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. **Lysosomes fuse** with the phagosome → lysosomal hydrolytic enzymes **digest** the pathogen. **[1]**
- ✓ 4. **Antigen fragments** from the digested pathogen are presented on the phagocyte's surface (MHC II) → acts as an **antigen-presenting cell (APC)**. **[1]**

**(b) APC definition and role. [2]** — *Explain*
- ✓ 1. An APC is a cell (e.g. macrophage) that **presents antigen fragments on its surface** (bound to MHC II molecules). **[1]**
- ✓ 2. It **activates T helper cells** by presenting the antigen, thus initiating the specific immune response. **[1]**

---

### B2
**(a) Events leading to antibody production after vaccination. [5]** — *Describe（流程）*
- ✓ 1. Vaccine contains **antigens** (or attenuated/killed pathogen). **[1]**
- ✓ 2. APCs present antigen → **T helper cells are activated** and release cytokines. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. Specific B cell is selected (**clonal selection**) by the antigen. **[1]**
- ✓ 4. B cell **proliferates (clonal expansion)** by mitosis. **[1]**
- ✓ 5. Differentiates into **plasma cells** (secrete antibodies) and **memory B cells**. **[1]**

**(b) Why better protected months later. [3]** — *Explain*
- ✓ 1. The vaccine induced production of **memory B cells** that persist for a long time. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. On re-exposure, memory cells **rapidly proliferate and differentiate** into plasma cells. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. This produces a **faster, larger antibody response** that neutralises the virus before disease develops. **[1]**
- ⚠️ 易错：只说"有抗体"——必须说明是**记忆细胞**驱动二次应答，不是第一次抗体还留着。

---

### B3
**(a) Distinguish humoral vs cell-mediated immunity. [4]** — *Distinguish（成对比较）*
- ✓ 1. Humoral involves **B lymphocytes** (and antibodies); cell-mediated involves **T lymphocytes** (Tc cells). **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Humoral targets **extracellular pathogens** (bacteria, free viruses, toxins); cell-mediated targets **intracellular pathogens** (virus-infected cells), tumours, and transplanted tissues. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. Humoral immunity acts through **secreted antibodies**; cell-mediated acts through **direct cell-to-cell contact** (Tc killing). **[1]**
- ✓ 4. Both involve **clonal selection and expansion** and produce **memory cells**. **[1]**

**(b) How Tc cells destroy virus-infected cells. [3]** — *Explain*
- ✓ 1. Tc cells recognise virus-derived antigen presented on the infected cell's surface (**MHC I + viral antigen**). **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Tc cell releases **perforin** → perforin inserts into the target cell membrane → **pores form**. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. Target cell **undergoes apoptosis** (programmed cell death) → virus cannot replicate further. **[1]**

---

### B4
**(a) Production of monoclonal antibodies. [5]** — *Describe（按顺序）*
- ✓ 1. Mouse is **immunised with the target antigen** → B cells producing specific antibodies form in spleen. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. **B cells are extracted** from the mouse's spleen. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. B cells are **fused with myeloma cells** (using PEG / polyethylene glycol) to form **hybridoma cells**. **[1]**
- ✓ 4. Hybridoma cells are selected (e.g. using HAT medium); screened to identify those producing the **desired antibody**. **[1]**
- ✓ 5. Selected hybridoma cells are **cultured in large quantities** → monoclonal antibodies are collected and purified. **[1]**

**(b) How a pregnancy test works. [3]** — *Explain*
- ✓ 1. Two different monoclonal antibodies specific to **hCG (human chorionic gonadotrophin)** are used. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. One antibody carries a **coloured label** (mobile); if hCG is present in urine, it binds this antibody and the complex moves along the strip. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. The complex is captured by the **second antibody immobilised on the test line** → a coloured band appears, indicating pregnancy. **[1]**

## Part C — 应用/数据题

### C1
**(a) Describe differences between response 1 and 2, with data. [3]** — *Describe（引用数据）*
- ✓ 1. After injection 2, antibody concentration rises **more rapidly** (shorter lag period). **[1]**
- ✓ 2. The **peak antibody concentration** is much higher after injection 2 than after injection 1. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. The antibody level remains **elevated for longer** after injection 2. **[1]**

**(b) Cellular basis for stronger secondary response. [3]** — *Explain*
- ✓ 1. The first injection produced **memory B cells** that persist in the blood. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. On second exposure, memory cells are **rapidly activated** and undergo **clonal expansion** much faster than naïve B cells. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. This produces a **larger population of plasma cells** → much greater antibody production. **[1]**

**(c) Why two injections may be needed. [2]** — *Suggest*
- ✓ 1. A single primary injection may produce **too few memory cells** for full protection. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. A booster dose ensures a **strong secondary response** and a **larger memory cell pool**, providing long-lasting immunity. **[1]**

---

### C2
**(a) Advantage of mAb over chemotherapy. [2]** — *Explain*
- ✓ 1. mAb **specifically targets cancer cells** by binding to receptor unique to them → **fewer side-effects** on normal cells. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. General chemotherapy **damages all rapidly dividing cells** (including normal cells like hair follicles, gut lining) → more side-effects. **[1]**

**(b) mAb conjugated to cytotoxic drug. [2]** — *Explain*
- ✓ 1. The mAb **delivers the drug directly to cancer cells** where the drug is released. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Concentration of cytotoxic drug at cancer cells is higher → **more effective killing**; less systemic toxicity. **[1]**

**(c) Problem if receptor also on normal cells. [1]** — *Suggest*
- ✓ mAb would **also bind to normal cells** bearing the receptor → cytotoxic drug would damage/kill normal cells → **toxic side-effects**. **[1]**

## 老师讲评建议 / Marking tips
- B2(a) 5 分题是高频大题，要求完整流程：**抗原 → APC → Th 细胞 → 克隆选择 → 克隆扩增 → 浆细胞 + 记忆细胞**，缺任一步丢分。
- B3(a) distinguish 题必须成对比较，不能只说 B 细胞的内容而不提 T 细胞。
- C1(a) 数据题：强制要求引用图中具体数值（如"Day X""peak concentration ~Y"）——只说"更高更快"不引数据扣分。
- 浆细胞 vs 记忆细胞：浆细胞短寿命、大量分泌抗体；记忆细胞长寿命、不分泌抗体（再次遇到抗原才激活）。
