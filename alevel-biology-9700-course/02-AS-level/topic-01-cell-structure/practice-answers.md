## Part B — 结构题（按采分点 ✓ 拆解）

### B1
**(a) Define resolution. [1]** — *command word: Define（给精准定义）*
- ✓ *The ability to distinguish (see as separate) two points/structures that are close together.* **[1]**

**(b) Explain why EM resolves what light microscope cannot. [2]** — *command word: Explain（讲原因）*
- ✓ 1. Electrons have a **much shorter wavelength** than light. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Shorter wavelength → can distinguish points that are closer together / higher resolution. **[1]**
- ⚠️ 易错：只说"电镜放大率更高"——**不得分**，问的是 resolution。

**(c) One limitation of EM. [1]** — *command word: State*
- ✓ 任一：specimen must be **dead / non-living**（真空、需脱水处理）／ preparation may create **artefacts** ／ very expensive ／ images not in natural colour. **[1]**

### B2（计算题）
**(a) Magnification of drawing. [2]** — *command word: Calculate（写步骤、统一单位）*
- ✓ 1. 换算并用公式：9 cm = 90 mm = 90 000 μm；M = I/A = 90 000 ÷ 60. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. = **×1500**（无单位）. **[1]**

**(b) Actual length of chloroplast. [2]**
- ✓ 1. A = I/M；6 mm = 6000 μm；A = 6000 ÷ 1500. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. = **4 μm**. **[1]**
- ⚠️ 放大率不带单位；实际大小**带单位**。

### B3
**(a) Identify W, X, Y, Z. [4]** — *command word: Identify*
- ✓ W = **Rough endoplasmic reticulum (RER)**
- ✓ X = **Golgi apparatus**
- ✓ Y = **Mitochondrion**
- ✓ Z = **Ribosome**
（每个 1 分）

**(b) Function of Y (mitochondrion). [2]** — *State*
- ✓ 1. Site of **aerobic respiration**. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. **Produces / synthesises ATP**. **[1]**
- ⚠️ 别写"产生能量(produces energy)"——要写**合成 ATP**。

**(c) How structure of Y suits function. [2]** — *Explain（结构→功能）*
- ✓ 1. Inner membrane folded into **cristae** → **large surface area**. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. More space for electron transport chain / ATP synthase / respiratory enzymes → more ATP. **[1]**

### B4
**(a) Outline secretion pathway. [4]** — *command word: Outline（按顺序给要点）*
- ✓ 1. Protein synthesised by **ribosomes on the RER**. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Packaged into a **vesicle** from RER, transported to **Golgi apparatus**. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. Golgi **modifies / processes** the protein and packages it into a **secretory vesicle**. **[1]**
- ✓ 4. Vesicle **fuses with the cell surface membrane**; protein released by **exocytosis**. **[1]**
- ⚠️ 易错：漏掉"囊泡运输"或"exocytosis"步骤。

**(b) Suggest why many mitochondria. [2]** — *Suggest（迁移）*
- ✓ 1. Exocytosis / transport / protein synthesis are **active processes requiring ATP**. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Mitochondria carry out **aerobic respiration to supply ATP**. **[1]**

### B5
**(a) Compare prokaryote vs eukaryote — 3 differences. [3]** — *Compare（必须成对！）*
任选三组，**每组成对**给分：
- ✓ Prokaryotes have **no membrane-bound nucleus** (DNA free in cytoplasm) **whereas** eukaryotes have a nucleus.
- ✓ Prokaryotes have **70S ribosomes whereas** eukaryotes have 80S.
- ✓ Prokaryotes have **no membrane-bound organelles whereas** eukaryotes do.
- ✓ Prokaryote cell wall is **peptidoglycan whereas** eukaryote (plant) is cellulose.
- ✓ Prokaryotes are **smaller** whereas eukaryotes are larger.
- ⚠️ 只写"原核有 X"不写"真核如何" → **不给分**（compare 必须成对）。

**(b) Why some antibiotics kill bacteria but not human cells. [2]** — *Explain*
- ✓ 1. Antibiotics target structures **unique to bacteria**, e.g. **70S ribosomes / peptidoglycan cell wall**. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Human cells **lack these** (have 80S ribosomes / no cell wall) so are not harmed. **[1]**

## Part C — 应用/数据题

### C1
**(a) Explain why protein still made but not secreted. [3]** — *Explain（用分泌途径原理）*
- ✓ 1. Protein is still synthesised on **ribosomes/RER**, which are unaffected → protein still made. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. The **Golgi** normally **modifies and packages** protein into secretory vesicles. **[1]**
- ✓ 3. With Golgi blocked, protein **cannot be packaged / transported to the membrane for exocytosis** → not secreted. **[1]**

**(b) One other process affected. [1]** — *Suggest*
- ✓ 任一：formation of **lysosomes** ／ production of **glycoproteins** ／ (in plants) cell-wall material. **[1]**

### C2（数据题）
**(a) How many times better. [2]** — *Calculate*
- ✓ 1. 200 ÷ 0.5. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. = **400 times** better. **[1]**

**(b) Which instrument resolves a 25 nm ribosome. [2]** — *Explain（引用数据！）*
- ✓ 1. Light microscope resolution 200 nm **> 25 nm**, so it **cannot** resolve a ribosome. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. Electron microscope resolution 0.5 nm **< 25 nm**, so it **can** resolve a ribosome. **[1]**
- 🎯 此题示范"**引用具体数值**"——不引数据（200/0.5/25）就丢分。

## Part D — 实验技能

### D1
**(a) Two rules for a good biological drawing. [2]** — *Describe*
任两条：
- ✓ Use **sharp pencil with clear, continuous single lines** (no sketchy/overlapping lines).
- ✓ **No shading** (use lines only).
- ✓ Draw **what you actually see**, in correct **proportion** (not a textbook diagram).
- ✓ **Label with ruled straight lines** that don't cross; include a **title / magnification**.

**(b) Average cell diameter in μm. [2]** — *Calculate*
- ✓ 1. Field of view 0.4 mm = **400 μm**; 400 ÷ 8. **[1]**
- ✓ 2. = **50 μm**. **[1]**

**(c) Why not shade darkly. [1]** — *Suggest*
- ✓ Shading **obscures detail / structures**; biological drawings should show structures with lines, not shading. **[1]**
