## Part A — 选择题 Multiple Choice（仿 Paper 1，每题 1 分）

**1.** Which statement about magnification and resolution is correct?
- A. Increasing magnification always increases resolution.
- B. Resolution is the number of times an image is enlarged.
- C. Resolution depends on the wavelength used to view the specimen.
- D. A light microscope has higher resolution than an electron microscope.

**2.** Why can a transmission electron microscope (TEM) resolve smaller structures than a light microscope?
- A. It uses a shorter wavelength of electrons.
- B. It magnifies the image more times.
- C. It uses coloured light.
- D. It can view living specimens.

**3.** A mitochondrion has an actual length of 8 μm. In a photomicrograph it measures 40 mm. What is the magnification?
- A. ×5
- B. ×200
- C. ×5000
- D. ×40000

**4.** Which organelle is the site of lipid synthesis?
- A. Rough endoplasmic reticulum
- B. Smooth endoplasmic reticulum
- C. Golgi apparatus
- D. Lysosome

**5.** Which structure is **not** surrounded by a membrane?
- A. Ribosome
- B. Lysosome
- C. Mitochondrion
- D. Nucleus

**6.** A cell is specialised to secrete large amounts of digestive enzyme. Which combination of organelles would be especially abundant?
- A. Chloroplasts and cell wall
- B. Rough ER, Golgi apparatus and mitochondria
- C. Centrioles and lysosomes
- D. Smooth ER and permanent vacuole

**7.** Which feature is found in a prokaryotic cell but **not** in a eukaryotic cell?
- A. 80S ribosomes
- B. A membrane-bound nucleus
- C. A cell wall made of peptidoglycan
- D. Mitochondria

**8.** Which row correctly compares ribosomes?
| | Prokaryote | Eukaryote (cytoplasm) |
|---|---|---|
| A | 80S | 70S |
| B | 70S | 80S |
| C | 70S | 70S |
| D | 80S | 80S |

**9.** Which is a correct statement about viruses?
- A. They contain both DNA and RNA.
- B. They have 70S ribosomes.
- C. They consist of nucleic acid surrounded by a protein capsid.
- D. They carry out respiration.

**10.** A scale bar labelled 5 μm measures 25 mm on a micrograph. What is the magnification?
- A. ×5
- B. ×200
- C. ×5000
- D. ×125000

## Part B — 结构题 Structured Questions（仿 Paper 2）

**1.** (a) Define the term *resolution*. **[1]**
   (b) Explain why an electron microscope can resolve two structures that a light microscope cannot. **[2]**
   (c) State **one** limitation of using an electron microscope to study cells. **[1]**

**2.** A plant cell is 60 μm long. A student draws it 9 cm long.
   (a) Calculate the magnification of the drawing. Show your working and give your answer to a suitable form. **[2]**
   (b) The student then draws a chloroplast in the same cell at the same magnification. The drawing of the chloroplast is 6 mm long. Calculate the actual length of the chloroplast in μm. **[2]**

**3.** The diagram shows an animal cell as seen with a TEM (organelles labelled W, X, Y, Z).
   *(W = structure with ribosomes on its surface; X = stack of flattened membrane sacs; Y = double-membdraned organelle with inner folds; Z = small structure with no membrane.)*
   (a) Identify organelles W, X, Y and Z. **[4]**
   (b) State the function of organelle Y. **[2]**
   (c) Explain how the structure of organelle Y is adapted to its function. **[2]**

**4.** (a) Outline how a protein, made for secretion, travels from its site of synthesis to the outside of the cell. **[4]**
   (b) Suggest why this type of cell contains many mitochondria. **[2]**

**5.** (a) Compare the structure of a prokaryotic cell with that of a eukaryotic cell. Give **three** differences. **[3]**
   (b) Explain why some antibiotics can kill bacteria without harming human cells. **[2]**

## Part C — 应用/数据题 Application（强化 AO2）

**1.** A scientist treated liver cells with a drug that blocks the Golgi apparatus. The cells continued to make protein but could no longer secrete it.
   (a) Using your knowledge of the secretion pathway, explain this observation. **[3]**
   (b) Suggest **one** other process in the cell that might be affected by a blocked Golgi apparatus. **[1]**

**2.** The table shows the resolution of three instruments.

| Instrument | Resolution / nm |
|---|---|
| Human eye | 100 000 |
| Light microscope | 200 |
| Electron microscope | 0.5 |

   (a) Calculate how many times better the resolution of the electron microscope is than the light microscope. Show your working. **[2]**
   (b) A ribosome is about 25 nm across. Explain which instrument(s) could resolve a single ribosome. **[2]**
