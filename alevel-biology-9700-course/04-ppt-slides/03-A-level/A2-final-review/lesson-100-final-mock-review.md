## Must-Know Equations 必背公式

**代谢与生态**：
$$\text{True photosynthesis} = \text{Apparent} + \text{Respiration}$$
$$\text{RQ} = \frac{\text{CO}_2 \text{ produced}}{\text{O}_2 \text{ consumed}}$$
$$D = 1 - \frac{\sum n(n-1)}{N(N-1)}$$

**遗传与统计**：
$$p + q = 1 \quad;\quad p^2 + 2pq + q^2 = 1$$
$$\chi^2 = \sum\frac{(O-E)^2}{E}$$
$$r_s = 1 - \frac{6\sum d^2}{n(n^2-1)}$$
$$N = \frac{MC}{R}$$
