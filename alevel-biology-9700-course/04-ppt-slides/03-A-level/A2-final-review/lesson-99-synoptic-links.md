## Practice Question

> **Compare** the roles of the **mitochondria** in aerobic respiration with the **chloroplast** in photosynthesis, focussing on electron transport and ATP synthesis. **[6]**

**A\* 答法（综合联系）**：
1. Both use **electron transport chains** in their membranes (inner mitochondrial / thylakoid membrane).
2. Electrons release energy → pump **H⁺** across membrane → concentration gradient.
3. **H⁺ flows through ATP synthase** → ATP synthesised by chemiosmosis (same mechanism, both).
4. In mitochondria, electrons from **NADH/FADH₂**; in chloroplasts, electrons from **water photolysis**.
5. Final electron acceptor: **O₂** (respiration) vs **NADP⁺** (photosynthesis).
